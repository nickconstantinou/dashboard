Who is the Best Captain for FPL Gameweek 37?

The Leading Contender

Bruno Fernandes currently occupies the top position in the Fantasy Premier League captaincy poll, commanding approximately 32% of votes cast by site users. For managers considering the Manchester United talisman, there are compelling performance metrics worth noting. Fernandes hasn't blanked at Old Trafford since Gameweek 12, providing a remarkably consistent home record that spans several months of the season. Additionally, the Portuguese midfielder needs just two assists to break the all-time Premier League assist record in a single season, adding a significant milestone narrative to his FPL appeal.

Arsenal's Attacking Duo

Bukayo Saka sits second in the captaincy poll, with Viktor Gyokeres occupying third position, as Arsenal's attacking pair draws strong support from the FPL community. Both players have started each of the last two Gameweeks and notched double-digit hauls in the Gunners' last match at the Emirates. This recent purple patch demonstrates their current involvement in Arsenal's attacking movements and their ability to deliver substantial point returns on home soil.

Saka is priced at £10.0m and represents the more established differential option, while Gyokeres offers an alternative at the same price point. More than 100,000 FPL managers have transferred each player ahead of Friday's deadline, underscoring the confidence the community places in Arsenal's offensive threat for this fixture.
