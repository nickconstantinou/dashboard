FPL Double Gameweek 33 And Blank Gameweek 34 Teams Confirmed: Six Clubs Set For Opposing Fixtures

The Full Story

Fantasy Premier League managers now have clarity on one of the most consequential fixture swings of the season. The complete line-up for Double Gameweek 33 and Blank Gameweek 34 has been finalised, with six Premier League clubs set to play twice in Gameweek 33 before all blanking in Gameweek 34.

The six teams confirmed for the double in Gameweek 33 are Leeds United, Bournemouth, Manchester City, Burnley, Chelsea, and Brighton and Hove Albion. Following their double fixtures, these same six clubs will then blank in Gameweek 34, creating a stark contrast in scheduling that will heavily influence FPL team selection and chip strategies over the coming weeks.

The announcement came from Fantasy Football Scout on April 6, 2026, confirming what many FPL managers had been anticipating as the Premier League fixture list continues to take shape through the business end of the season.

Why This Matters

This fixture configuration represents a critical strategic crossroads for FPL managers. The six teams with double fixtures in Gameweek 33 will offer obvious appeal for managers looking to maximize returns from players with two matches in a single gameweek. However, the subsequent blank in Gameweek 34 means managers must weigh whether investing heavily in these double-gameweek players is worth the guaranteed zero returns they will receive the following week.

The timing is particularly significant because many FPL managers will be developing strategies around their bench boost and free hit chips, trying to navigate the blank gameweek while maintaining competitive squads. Manchester City players, in particular, tend to be heavily owned given their consistent scoring potential, meaning a large portion of the FPL player base will need to find solutions for their blank in Gameweek 34.

This fixture swing also impacts captaincy decisions significantly, as managers consider whether to captain double-gameweek players in Gameweek 33 or save the armband for teams with favorable fixtures in Gameweek 34.
