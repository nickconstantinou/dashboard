How FPL Harry, Mark Sutherns + More Did in Gameweek 33

Chip Deployments

Gameweek 33 saw several top managers making strategic chip plays. Nine managers activated their Bench Boost, and all nine cleared 100 points. Fabio Borges and Luke Williams stood out among Bench Boost users, each hauling a substantial 30 points from their bench assets.

Seven managers opted for the Free Hit chip. However, only Huss E and FPL Frazier managed to reach triple digits with this approach.

Double Gameweek Opportunities

The fixture schedule presented double Gameweek 33 with six teams featuring double fixtures. Players from Manchester City, Chelsea, Bournemouth, Brighton and Hove Albion, Burnley, and Leeds United all had the opportunity to play twice.

Injury Updates

Two notable players entered the injury list: Joao Pedro (£7.7m) and Cole Palmer (£10.5m). This created difficult decisions for managers weighing transfers against holding injured assets.

Transfer Activity

The template remained heavily concentrated, with 11 of the 15 players boasting over 75% ownership. Several managers made targeted transfers based on the fixture swings.

Gabriel was a popular exit, with James Hill (£4.2m) stepping in as the budget replacement. Mark Sutherns and Tom Dollimore both transferred Joao Pedro in for Jarrod Bowen (£7.7m).

Last Week's Fallen Stars

The players dropped the previous week included Gabriel, Bowen, Martin Dubravka (£4.0m), Virgil van Dijk (£6.2m), and Igor Thiago (£7.4m).

Top Performers

Pingreen and Luke Williams delivered identical huge returns, each scoring 134 points with the same squad selections. This demonstrated that particular strategies yielded exceptional results when executed correctly.
