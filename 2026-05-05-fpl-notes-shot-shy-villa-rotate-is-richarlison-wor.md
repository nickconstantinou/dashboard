# FPL Notes: Shot-Shy Villa Rotate + Is Richarlison Worth a Punt?

## Tottenham's Valuable Assets Deliver

Tottenham Hotspur gave their survival prospects a huge boost with an impressive away victory at Aston Villa on Sunday night. The north London side secured a 2-1 win, with goals from Conor Gallagher and Richarlison settling the match. Conor Gallagher, priced at £5.0m, found the net for Spurs, while Richarlison at £6.3m also contributed to the attacking returns. Both players represent budget-friendly options in the Tottenham midfield and forward lines respectively.

## Aston Villa's Rotation and Injury Concerns

Manager Unai Emery made several notable selection decisions for the match. The Spaniard made a calculated gamble to omit Ollie Watkins from the starting XI. The £8.8m forward was joined on the bench by Paul Torres, Ezri Konsa, and Lucas Digne. Beyond the rotation choices, Aston Villa also faced genuine injury problems. Amadou Onana, priced at £4.8m, and John McGinn, at £5.3m, were both unavailable due to injury. McGinn's absence was particularly unexpected given his importance to the side.

## Villa's Offensive Struggles

The match highlighted significant concerns about Villa's attacking output. Aston Villa had no shots until the hour mark, demonstrating a severe lack of creative intent throughout the opening hour. They failed to register a shot on target until the 96th minute. Morgan Rogers, valued at £7.5m, had two efforts during the match, but both were long-range attempts taken from outside the box. Both shots were blocked, resulting in a combined Statsbomb xG of just 0.04. This performance underscores the difficulties Villa faced in creating meaningful chances.

## Looking Ahead

The expectation is that both McGinn and Watkins will start when Aston Villa face Forest on Thursday. This provides relief for managers who have invested in Villa assets, as key players should return to the lineup. However, Fantasy managers should also consider that the Europa League final falls between Gameweeks 37 and 38, which could impact Aston Villa's end-of-season planning given their European commitments this campaign.
