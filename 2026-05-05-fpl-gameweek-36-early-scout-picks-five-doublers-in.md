# FPL Gameweek 36 Early Scout Picks: Five 'Doublers' Included

## Man City Assets Leading the Way

With five teams set to play twice in Gameweek 36, Fantasy Football Scout has identified key players to consider for early Scout Picks.

Rayan Cherki (£6.5m) has been in superb form, giving him a clear head start over Antoine Semenyo (£8.1m) in the race for Scout Picks inclusion. The French midfielder offers exceptional value at his price point.

Alongside Cherki, Nico O'Reilly (£5.2m) and Erling Haaland (£14.6m) are no-brainer selections for managers looking to maximise their Gameweek 36 returns. Both Man City assets provide strong template options at their respective price brackets.

## Brighton's Favourable Fixture

Brighton and Hove Albion have a very presentable home fixture against already-relegated Wolverhampton Wanderers. Jack Hinshelwood has been very threatening from open play since switching to a No 10 role, making him an attractive budget option at £5.1m for managers seeking differential picks.

## Crystal Palace Rotation Watch

Crystal Palace are in midweek European action, with the primary focus for them now being the Conference League. Dean Henderson (£5.1m) and Maxence Lacroix (£5.2m), for now at least, are the leading contenders from Palace for Scout Picks consideration.

Daniel Munoz (£5.9m) has already scored against Everton this season, though he was hooked at half-time on Sunday in a pre-planned substitution. Oliver Glasner rotated five players out on Sunday, so midweek minutes will be vital in determining final Scout Picks for Crystal Palace assets.
