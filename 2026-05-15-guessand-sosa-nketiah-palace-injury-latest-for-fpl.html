Palace Injury Latest for FPL Gameweek 37: Guessand Returns, Nketiah Remains Out

Evann Guessand Set for Involvement

Crystal Palace attacker Evann Guessand is back in training and set to be involved for Gameweek 37. The Aston Villa loanee has been working his way back from a knee injury, and manager Oliver Glasner hopes Guessand returns to the squad for Sunday. The 22-year-old is priced at £6.2m in FPL and could provide budget-friendly attacking options if he gets the nod.

Eddie Nketiah Remains Unavailable

Eddie Nketiah continues his spell on the sidelines after sustaining a hamstring injury. The £5.4m forward remains out for this fixture, leaving Palace without one of their summer signings for the time being.

Borna Sosa Expected Back Next Gameweek

Borna Sosa's return looks set to come in Gameweek 38 rather than this round. The Croatian defender, valued at £4.8m, is dealing with an unspecified issue, and his absence could last until Arsenal. Fantasy managers considering the full-back should plan for an additional week without him.

Cheick Doucoure Still Out

Cheick Doucoure is also unavailable, with the midfielder's knee/muscle/match fitness concerns keeping him sidelined alongside Nketiah.

Squad Fitness Assessment

Beyond the names mentioned, everyone else is fit at Crystal Palace. This gives Oliver Glasner a relatively clean bill of health to work with outside of the three unavailable players.
