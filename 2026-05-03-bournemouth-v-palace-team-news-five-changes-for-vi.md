# Bournemouth v Palace: Five Changes for Visitors

Crystal Palace have made five changes to their starting lineup for their Premier League fixture against Bournemouth. The Eagles have one eye on the second leg of their UEFA Conference League semi-final against Shakhtar Donetsk on Thursday, prompting significant rotation.

## Palace Squad Adjustments

Five players who featured in Palace's recent matches have been demoted to substitute duty. Chris Richards, Tyrick Mitchell, Adam Wharton, Ismaila Sarr and Jean-Philippe Mateta will begin the match on the bench.

They are replaced in the starting XI by five players coming into the side. Chadi Riad, Justin Devenny, Jefferson Lerma, Brennan Johnson and Jorgen Strand Larsen have all been promoted from their recent substitute roles.

## Bournemouth Lineup Changes

On the opposite side, Bournemouth boss Andoni Iraola has made alterations to his starting eleven. Tyler Adams and Rayan have been brought into the lineup for the hosts.

Ryan Christie and David Brooks have dropped to the bench as a result of these changes.
