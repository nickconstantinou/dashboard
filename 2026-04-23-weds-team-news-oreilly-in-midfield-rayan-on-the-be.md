# Team News and Lineup Notes

## Manchester City

Nico O'Reilly has moved into central midfield for Manchester City. The shift appears linked to Rodri being slightly injured, with Rayan Ait-Nouri brought in to fill that void in the side. Erling Haaland starts for Manchester City at his price of £14.5m. O'Reilly is available at £5.1m.

## Bournemouth

Bournemouth made one alteration to their lineup, bringing in David Brooks for Rayan. Antoine Semenyo continues his starting role for the club at £8.2m.

## Leeds United

Leeds United have kept faith with the same XI that secured a 3-0 victory over Wolverhampton Wanderers on Saturday. Joe Rodon begins on the bench for Leeds at £3.9m. Dominic Calvert-Lewin leads the line at £5.7m.

## Burnley

Burnley made three adjustments to their side. Josh Laurent, Bashir Humphreys, and Loum Tchaouna came in for Marcus Edwards, Lesley Ugochukwu, and Florentino Luis.
