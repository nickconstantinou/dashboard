# Leeds Injury Latest for FPL Gameweek 36

*Okafor, Struijk, and Gudmundsson remain concerns heading into a pivotal week for Leeds United's Premier League survival hopes.*

---

## Leeds United Injury List Overview

Leeds United heading into Gameweek 36 face a significant injury list affecting several Fantasy Premier League assets. The club's preparation for the period has been complicated by manager Daniel Farke being ill, with Edmund Riemer sitting in for him at the press conference.

---

## Noah Okafor (£5.6m) – Calf Problem

Noah Okafor is currently unavailable due to a calf injury and will likely miss the next two games. The forward sustained an injury after the FA Cup game, where he suffered a right calf contusion. Additionally, it has been reported that Okafor pulled his left calf during the Burnley game. This suggests a dual calf issue for the player.

Despite these setbacks, Leeds United remain hopeful that Okafor will be available for the last game of the season.

---

## Pascal Struijk (£4.4m) – Hip Contusion

Pascal Struijk has a hip contusion. The defender will train individually but Leeds are hopeful of his recovery for Monday's match. For FPL managers considering Struijk as a defensive option, his availability for the upcoming fixture remains uncertain but carries some optimism from the club.

---

## Gabriel Gudmundsson (£3.8m) – Hamstring Injury

Gabriel Gudmundsson has a hamstring injury and will be out for this week. The club has indicated they need to assess him on a daily basis following this setback. Gudmundsson's injury means FPL managers should look elsewhere for value options in his price bracket for the current gameweek.

---

## Ilia Gruev (£4.7m) – Hamstring Issue

Ilia Gruev has also been added to the Leeds United injury list with a hamstring problem. This represents a further setback to Leeds' midfield options as the season reaches its conclusion.

---

## Context: Leeds' Survival Battle

Leeds United's Premier League survival could be secured before a ball is kicked on Monday due to Arsenal playing West Ham. However, the club has stated they will prepare for their match as if a result will be needed regardless of other results, with Edmund Riemer confirming the approach: "We prepare in the best possible way. Of course, we take it however it comes but we want to be ready, whatever happens."

---

## FPL Manager Considerations

For Gameweek 36, FPL managers holding Okafor, Gudmundsson, or Gruev should plan for their absence. Struijk represents a possible rotation option if he completes individual training and is deemed fit for Monday's match, though his participation remains contingent on his recovery from the hip contusion.
