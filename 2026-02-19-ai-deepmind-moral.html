Google DeepMind: Are Chatbots Just Virtue Signaling?

Google DeepMind is calling for rigorous evaluation of large language models' moral reasoning capabilities. Researchers want to distinguish between genuine ethical understanding and mere performance.

Key Findings

Studies reveal LLMs can dramatically change moral stances based on minor formatting changes or user disagreement. This suggests their ethical responses may be superficial rather than deeply reasoned.

From MIT Tech Review:
