# FPL Gameweek 34 Team News: Friday's Live Injury Updates

This article summarises the confirmed and potential injury situations from Friday's live updates ahead of Fantasy Premier League Gameweek 34.

## Liverpool

**Goalkeeping Uncertainty**

Alisson remains a doubt for Liverpool's Saturday match against Crystal Palace. The Brazilian goalkeeper has not been pictured in full team training on Wednesday and has been nursing a muscle injury. However, he has not yet been ruled out of the fixture entirely.

Manager Arne Slot suggested that Freddie Woodman would step in as the starting goalkeeper if Alisson is not ready to start. Woodman, priced at £3.9m, would be the immediate replacement option for FPL managers seeking cover between the posts.

**Defensive Boost**

Joe Gomez has returned to the squad after a brief absence. Slot described the defender's return as "really positive" given that last week he only had two centre-backs available with no substitutes on the bench. Gomez is priced at £4.9m and could provide rotation value.

**Mamardashvili Ruled Out**

Giorgi Mamardashvili will definitely miss the visit of Crystal Palace this weekend. The goalkeeper, priced at £4.1m, is also expected to be unavailable for weeks beyond the current match due to a leg wound.

## Arsenal

**Saka and Calafiori Nearing Returns**

Bukayo Saka looks set to be included in Arsenal's squad this weekend. The winger, valued at £9.8m, would provide a significant boost to attacking options if fit enough to feature. Manager Mikel Arteta described the news as "good."

Riccardo Calafiori is also likely to be in contention for the matchday squad. The defender, priced at £5.6m, appears close to returning from his injury lay-off.

**Timber Remains Absent**

Jurrien Timber will not be in Arsenal's squad this weekend. The Dutch defender continues his recovery and remains unavailable for selection.

## Tottenham Hotspur

**Udogie Sidelined**

Destiny Udogie cannot play for Tottenham tomorrow due to a muscle injury. The left-back, priced at £4.3m, is unavailable for the upcoming fixture.

**Vicario Still Unavailable**

Guglielmo Vicario has not recovered from hernia surgery and is not ready to return. The goalkeeper, valued at £4.7m, continues his rehabilitation alongside Pape Matar Sarr, who is also sidelined with a shoulder injury.

## Manchester United

**Endo Timeline Clarified**

Wataru Endo will not be available for Manchester United next week or the week following. However, the midfielder is making progress in his recovery and is expected to return at the end of the season. He is currently completing rehabilitation exercises outside on the pitch with the medical team.
