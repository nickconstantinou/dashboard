Everton v Man City team news: Semenyo starts, no Rodri

📖 Read original on Fantasy Football Scout →

Key Lineup Changes

Everton Starting XI

Tim Iroegbunam receives a rare start for Everton in this fixture. The midfielder is priced at £4.8m for Fantasy Premier League purposes.

Also earning an infrequent opportunity is Merlin Rohl, who gets a start alongside Iroegbunam in Everton's midfield.

On the opposite end of the selection spectrum, Dwight McNeil drops to the bench for this match. McNeil carries an FPL price of £5.5m.

Veteran midfielder Idrissa Gueye misses out entirely from Everton's matchday squad.

Manchester City Starting XI

Antoine Semenyo starts for Manchester City in this Gameweek 35 fixture. The forward, priced at £8.1m, returns to the XI after being absent in the FA Cup semi-final.

One change from recent matches sees Nico Gonzalez enter the starting lineup, replacing Rayan Ait-Nouri in the side.

Rodri remains unavailable and is not involved again for the reigning champions.

Fixture Context

This match concludes Gameweek 35 of the Fantasy Premier League season. Kick-off is scheduled for 20:00 BST.

Pep Guardiola, Manchester City's manager, has largely reverted to his strongest available side for this encounter.
