FPL notes: Salah injury latest, Isak goal + Palace impress

📖 Read original on Fantasy Football Scout →

Salah Injury Update

Mohamed Salah (£14.0m) was forced off during Liverpool's 3-1 victory over Crystal Palace, clutching his hamstring just before the hour mark. Liverpool manager Arne Slot stated it was too early to determine the full extent of the injury. Despite this concern, Salah was brought in by over 750,000 managers ahead of Gameweek 34, indicating many Fantasy managers remain optimistic about his prospects.

Isak Scores at Anfield

Alexander Isak (£10.3m) scored his first league goal at Anfield on Saturday—though notably, this came while playing for Newcastle United against Liverpool. Up until that match, Isak had actually scored more league goals at Anfield for Newcastle United than Liverpool had managed in the same fixture.

Isak completed 78 minutes in this match, representing his longest run-out since returning from injury. However, his goal was his only attempt on target all game, and he had struggled to influence proceedings before breaking the deadlock.

Crystal Palace's Attacking Performance

Crystal Palace produced a statistically impressive attacking display despite the 3-1 defeat at Anfield. The Eagles outshot Liverpool 14-9 and outperformed them in expected goals, posting an xG of 2.26 compared to Liverpool's 1.15. Palace generated five big chances but converted none of them, while Liverpool created three big chances and scored from all three.

Other FPL Notes

Curtis Jones (£5.4m) earned a second successive start at right-back and delivered a solid performance, mirroring his display in the Merseyside derby.

Dominik Szoboszlai (£7.1m) recorded his second-fewest number of final-third touches this season, finishing with just 13 touches in the attacking zone.
