Hall, Joelinton, Gordon: Newcastle injury latest for FPL Gameweek 37

Newcastle Injury Update: Hall Fit, Joelinton Doubt, Gordon Exit Imminent?

Lewis Hall Returns to Fitness

Lewis Hall appears set for a return to Fantasy Premier League action this Gameweek. According to comments from Eddie Howe, Hall has trained this week and is available for selection. The defender carries a price tag of £5.3m, making him a budget option in Newcastle's defensive line.

Anthony Gordon: Summer Exit Looms

For FPL managers considering Anthony Gordon, a significant factor has emerged. Eddie Howe has hinted that Gordon is likely to leave Newcastle during the summer transfer window. When questioned about Gordon's recent omissions from the starting lineup, Howe suggested the club is beginning to plan with the future in mind. Gordon is priced at £7.3m in FPL.

Joelinton: Thigh Concern

Another midfielder of interest faces uncertainty ahead of this weekend. Joelinton has picked up a slight thigh problem and has been marked as a doubt by Howe. The manager stated the club will assess him in the coming days, leaving managers with a decision to make before the Gameweek deadline.

Long-Term Absences Continue

Several Newcastle players remain unavailable and offer no immediate FPL value. Lewis Miley continues his recovery from a foot injury, Emil Krafth remains sidelined with a knee problem, and Tino Livramento is working his way back from a thigh issue. Fabian Schar, who has been battling an ankle and foot problem, will not feature in Newcastle's final two matches of the season.
