FPL Blank Gameweek 34: Are Any Spurs Players Worth Buying?

The De Zerbi Effect

Tottenham Hotspur appointed Roberto De Zerbi as manager in Gameweek 32, a move that brought immediate tactical changes to the squad. Under his leadership, Tottenham have played a 4-3-3 formation, with the team showing considerable improvement in their most recent outing. Against Brighton, the side registered 13 shots and created three Opta 'big chances', suggesting an evolving attacking approach that FPL managers should monitor.

Key Tottenham Assets

Pedro Porro (£6.5m)

Porro stands out as a potentially valuable option under De Zerbi. Under the previous manager, Tudor removed him from set-piece duties, but those responsibilities have been restored with De Zerbi's arrival. Additionally, Porro leads the way for chances created under the new manager, with four chances created. His dual threat on set plays and open play chance creation makes him a notable option.

Conor Gallagher (£5.0m)

Gallagher occupied the most advanced central midfielder role against Brighton, racking up 10 penalty box touches—more than any other player in that position. This positioning suggests goal-threat potential at an accessible price point.

Guglielmo Vicario (£4.7m)

For managers seeking budget options, Vicario is due back from hernia surgery soon and should reclaim the number one spot. His return would provide a solid goalkeeping option at a modest price.

Upcoming Fixtures

Tottenham have home matches against Leeds and Everton following Gameweek 34. These fixtures represent potentially favourable attacking opportunities, which could make Porro and other attacking assets more appealing.

Bottom Line

Spurs' upcoming home fixtures against Leeds and Everton, combined with their improved attacking output under De Zerbi, suggest potential value in Tottenham assets. Porro's restored set-piece duties and chance-creation output make him particularly noteworthy, while Gallagher's advanced positioning offers budget-friendly excitement. Managers should watch for consistent minutes under the new system before committing.
