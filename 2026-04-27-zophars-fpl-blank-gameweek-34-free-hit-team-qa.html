Overview

Fantasy Premier League managers face their biggest challenge of the season in Gameweek 34, which is the biggest blank gameweek of the season. With limited options available, many managers are turning to strategic chip usage to navigate this difficult fixture period.

About the Author

Zophar is an eight-time top 10k finisher in Fantasy Premier League, establishing him as one of the most experienced and successful managers in the FPL community. His track record demonstrates consistent performance over multiple seasons, making his insights particularly valuable for managers seeking guidance.

Free Hit Strategy

In this article, Zophar reveals his approach to navigating Gameweek 34. Most FPL managers will be activating their Free Hit chip this week, including Zophar himself. The Free Hit chip allows managers to field a completely new team for a single gameweek while retaining their original squad for future gameweeks, making it an ideal tool for blank gameweeks where fewer players are available.

One notable question came from a user named SIMBOBIANTHEIII, who asked about non-negotiable Free Hit picks—specifically inquiring who would be 100% guaranteed selections on a Free Hit team. This question highlights a common concern among managers trying to maximize their Free Hit investment during the blank gameweek.
