Chelsea Injury Latest for FPL Gameweek 37: Neto, Sanchez, Garnacho Updates

Robert Sanchez Fit for Selection

Robert Sanchez is fit to start and available for selection for Gameweek 37. The goalkeeper sat out Chelsea's draw with Liverpool due to a head injury but has recovered well. According to reports, he's trained well this week and the club is "really hopeful looking into Saturday."

Sanchez's return provides a boost to Fantasy Premier League managers considering the Chelsea backline, as the team prepares for their final league fixture before the FA Cup final.

Pedro Neto Returns to Training

Pedro Neto has returned to training after missing the last two Gameweeks with knocks. The winger could feature in Saturday's FA Cup final, having trained well this week. Reports indicate he is "looking really good" and the club is "really hopeful" regarding his availability.

Alejandro Garnacho Back in Training

Alejandro Garnacho has also returned to training after missing the last two Gameweeks with knocks. Like Neto, the winger could feature in Saturday's FA Cup final. Sources confirm that Garnacho has trained well this week and the club is optimistic about his potential involvement.

Reece James Available After Successful Return

Reece James came through his Gameweek 36 appearance and another week of training unscathed after returning from injury. The defender, priced at £5.6m, has been monitored carefully following his comeback. Reports indicate he's trained well and the club is happy with his progress. His performance upon his return was described positively, with the player getting valuable minutes under his belt.

Levi Colwill Cleared After Gameweek 36

Levi Colwill also came through his Gameweek 36 appearance and another week of training unscathed after returning from injury. The defender has completed a "really good rehab" and had a successful training week. His display against Liverpool was described as "exceptional," providing Fantasy managers with confidence in his availability moving forward.

Players Still Unavailable

Several Chelsea players remain unavailable for Gameweek 37:

Jesse Derry is still out with a head injury. The midfielder, priced at £4.5m, continues his recovery and was not included in the latest squad update.

Estevao remains sidelined with a hamstring injury and will not be available for the upcoming fixture.

Jamie Gittens is still out with a hamstring injury. To the best of available knowledge, the player continues his rehabilitation and has not yet returned to training.
