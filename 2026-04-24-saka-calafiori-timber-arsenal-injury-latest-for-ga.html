Arsenal Injury Latest for Gameweek 34

Team News

Arsenal head into Gameweek 34 with no new injury concerns, according to manager Mikel Arteta. The Gunners appear to have emerged from their recent fixtures without adding to their existing injury list.

Bukayo Saka

Bukayo Saka is expected to be available for selection this weekend. The £9.8m midfielder, who has been managing an Achilles issue, could return to the squad according to Arteta. Speaking to the media, Arteta stated: "Bukayo probably is going to be in the squad, so good news." This represents a positive update for Fantasy managers who have held the England international through his recent absence.

Riccardo Calafiori

Riccardo Calafiori is also likely to feature in the squad for the upcoming fixture. The £5.6m defender has been nursing an unspecified injury but appears close to a return. Arteta indicated: "[Riccardo] probably in the squad, as well." Fantasy managers priced Calafiori at £5.6m may want to monitor his participation in training ahead of the Gameweek deadline.

Jurrien Timber

Jurrien Timber will not be available for this weekend's match. The Dutch defender has been sidelined with an ankle issue and remains out, with Arteta confirming: "Jurrien not in the squad." Managers will need to continue without the £5.0m defender for another Gameweek at minimum.

Mikel Merino

Mikel Merino continues his recovery from a foot injury and remains a longer-term absentee. The midfielder is not expected to return in the near future, with his absence extending beyond this Gameweek. Managers should not factor Merino into their plans for the immediate run of fixtures.
