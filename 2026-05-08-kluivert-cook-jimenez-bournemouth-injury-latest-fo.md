# Bournemouth Injury Update: Kluivert Close to Return, Cook Targeting Final Fixtures

## Justin Kluivert Nearing Comeback

Justin Kluivert looks set to make his return to the Bournemouth matchday squad for the first time in over four months. The midfielder has been back in training over the last fortnight following a lengthy layoff. Kluivert underwent knee surgery in January and has been out of action since that procedure. Fantasy managers have been eagerly awaiting his return, and his availability at a price of £6.9m will be a welcome boost heading into the closing stages of the season.

## Lewis Cook on Recovery Track

Lewis Cook's return appears to be slightly behind Kluivert's in the rehabilitation process. Cook has been training, though not fully, and is working his way back from a hamstring and calf issue. Manager Andoni Iraola has indicated that Cook is expected to return in the final two Gameweeks of the season. However, he is unlikely to feature against Fulham in the upcoming match. The Bournemouth boss expressed cautious optimism, stating that while Cook probably will not be ready for tomorrow's fixture, he hopes both players can be available for the last two games of the campaign.

## No Other Concerns Beyond Usual Niggles

Iraola reported no other significant injury concerns within the Bournemouth squad beyond the usual knocks and niggles that accumulate throughout a grueling season. The manager acknowledged that the Cherries, like all teams at this point in the campaign, are managing some minor issues but have no fresh major concerns to address.

## Alex Jimenez Remains Unavailable

Alex Jimenez will not feature against Fulham, with the club currently investigating further after social media posts emerged concerning the right-back. Fantasy managers should note that Jimenez is unavailable for the immediate fixture, and his situation warrants monitoring for any updates regarding the investigation.

## Fantasy Implications

For FPL managers weighing their Gameweek 36 transfers and captaincy choices, Kluivert's potential return provides an interesting budget option at £6.9m. Cook remains a longer-term prospect targeting the season's final fixtures. Jimenez owners will need to seek alternatives for the Fulham match, with no clarity on when he might return to action.
