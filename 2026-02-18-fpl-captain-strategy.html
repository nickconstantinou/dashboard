The Captain Framework

1. The Premium Pick (Salah, Haaland, Palmer)

These are your set-and-forget captains. When in doubt, captain the premium player with a good fixture. They score regardless of match context.

2. The Differential Captain

Sometimes you captain a player owned by <10% of managers. If they haul, you gain massive rank. If they blank, you haven't lost much vs. the template.

3. The Form Player

Captain based on current form, not reputation. A player on 3 consecutive hauls > player on 1 big score.

When to Wildcard the Armband

  • Blank Gameweeks: Captain a player with double fixtures
  • Double Gameweeks: Target players with 2 matches - 2x points potential
  • Fixture swings: When your captain has tough fixtures, consider the alternative

Pro Tips

  • Check effective ownership - if >60% own them, the haul is priced in
  • Look at xG + xA, not just goals
  • Fixture difficulty rating (FDR) matters but form beats fixtures
  • The captain analyzer tool helps track your success rate vs other managers
