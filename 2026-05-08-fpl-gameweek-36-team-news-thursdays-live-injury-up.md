# FPL Gameweek 36 Team News: Thursday's Live Injury Updates

This article provides verified and reported injury updates from Gameweek 36, sourced from Fantasy Football Scout's Thursday team news coverage.

## Chelsea

Chelsea faces Liverpool in a key Gameweek 36 fixture. Reece James (£5.6m) and Levi Colwill have returned to training and are in contention for starting roles. Both players returned to the bench in Gameweek 35 and have trained fully throughout the week. They could even start against Liverpool.

However, Chelsea will be without goalkeeper Robert Sanchez after he suffered a head injury in the defeat to Nottingham Forest on Monday. Sanchez will miss Gameweek 36 as a result.

Additionally, Jesse Derry's campaign is over after he suffered a head injury in the same Nottingham Forest game. Derry was priced at £4.5m.

## Manchester United

Matthijs de Ligt (£4.9m) remains unavailable due to a back injury and will continue his absence.

On a positive note, Lisandro Martinez is available again after serving a three-match ban and could return to contention.

Meanwhile, Pedro Neto and Alejandro Garnacho are both carrying knocks and remain absent from training. Both players were absent from the recent 1-3 defeat and are unlikely to feature in Gameweek 36.

## Tottenham Hotspur

Guglielmo Vicario (£4.7m) is not yet ready to return from his hernia injury, with the Spurs goalkeeper remaining sidelined.

## Bournemouth

Dominic Solanke is improving but is expected to return in Gameweek 37 rather than Gameweek 36. The forward is recovering from a hamstring injury and is not completely ruled out, though the next fixture at Stamford Bridge appears more likely for his comeback.

## Summary

Key absentees continuing through Gameweek 36 include Matthijs de Ligt, Guglielmo Vicario, Robert Sanchez, Pedro Neto, and Alejandro Garnacho. Reece James and Levi Colwill represent potential differentials for Chelsea if selected against Liverpool, while Lisandro Martinez returns to United's available pool.
