The latest FFS Members Cup results + second-round draw

📖 Read original on Fantasy Football Scout →

The Fantasy Football Scout Members Cup has delivered a dramatic opening act, with the first round concluding in Gameweek 31 and the draw for round two now confirmed. Of the 128 managers who survived the qualifying phase, a significant number of them have already been eliminated, including four of the six former champions who entered the competition. Mayanyi and Drizzle emerged as the sole former winners to progress past the first round, while Mohd Rodzi, Rossaldinho, rrcmc, and Chabs all suffered early exits at the hands of BilboBaggins, RamaJama, Joletouriste, and IvanGeorgiev respectively. The competition now shifts to Gameweek 32, where the remaining field will compete for a share of £300 in prizes, with the ultimate winner securing a £150 Amazon voucher.

The first round showcased the unpredictable nature of FPL cups, where raw point totals often prove more decisive than season-long pedigree. FplSos and Ryan with Podnah and Wyrd bið ful aræd led all scorers with 76 points each, while Surgical Attack and DrMammon advanced comfortably on 75 points. However, the cruel lottery of the cup draw meant that Flying Nugget and norab were eliminated despite posting 69 points—a tally that would have been sufficient for advancement in most other matchups. At the other end of the spectrum, pk all stars and 100K Short survived with just 50 points, demonstrating how opponent quality matters more than absolute scores in knockout formats. Five additional managers—ruddjack, FPL Virgin, Milvus, Ckeulen, and Johnny 8—moved through on 52 points.

Perhaps most intriguingly, the competition proved that overall rank provides little protection in head-to-head cup formats. Matthias99th, sitting at 1,482nd in the overall FPL rankings, secured advancement and now faces oscardude1 (16,140th) in what promises to be the most compelling second-round tie given the combined low ranks of both managers. Conversely, Pulisic 4 President (1,492nd), YNWA91 (1,511th), FPL Niall (3,251st), and Toalie (3,477th) all exited despite strong overall rankings, illustrating how the cup format creates equal-opportunity elimination regardless of season performance. Even more remarkably, ProfessorM—ranked down at 4.39 million—has survived to round two and will face The Great statuto (1.99m), proving that anything remains possible in this tournament structure.

The FFS Members Cup operates under rules nearly identical to the official FPL cup game: managers are randomly drawn against opponents, and the winner is whoever scores the highest in a given Gameweek after subtracting any points spent on transfers. Ties are resolved by referring to the overall FPL rankings, with the higher-ranked manager advancing. The competition will follow a rapid-fire schedule through the remainder of the season, with round three in Gameweek 33, round four in Gameweek 34, and round five in Gameweek 35, before quarter-finals in Gameweek 36, semi-finals in Gameweek 37, and the final plus third-place playoff in Gameweek 38. With £150 for first place, £100 for second, and £50 for third, the stakes will only intensify as the field narrows toward the season's conclusion.
