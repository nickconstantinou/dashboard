FPL Analysis: Doku States His Double Gameweek 36 Case

Jeremy Doku's Recent FPL Returns

Jeremy Doku (£6.4m) has emerged as a compelling FPL asset ahead of Double Gameweek 36. The Manchester City winger scored twice in Matchweek 35, breaking the deadlock and then rescuing a point in the 97th minute. This brace demonstrates his ability to both open scoring and deliver match-winning contributions in closing minutes.

Beyond his goal contributions, Doku created a match-high four chances against Everton. He is now boasting four attacking returns from four successive starts, highlighting consistent involvement in City attacks throughout recent fixtures.

Manchester City Team News

The midfield situation remains a factor for City managers to consider. Rodri (£6.3m) was missing through injury again, with the club forced to deploy Nico Gonzalez (£5.9m) in the Rodri-shaped hole in central midfield during recent matches.

Defensively, both Ruben Dias (£5.5m) and Josko Gvardiol (£5.8m) remain sidelined through injury, potentially affecting City's defensive stability in the coming fixtures.

Erling Haaland's Scoring Form

Erling Haaland (£14.6m) continues his prolific output this season. The City frontman reached his 25th Premier League goal of the campaign and has now scored in three straight league matches. His consistent finishing provides City with their primary goal threat.

Everton Defensive Concerns

For managers targeting Everton's fixtures, the Toffees have now shipped nine goals in four matches. This defensive vulnerability may present opportunities for City's attackers to exploit.
