FPL Gameweek 35 team news: Wednesday’s live injury updates

📖 Read original on Fantasy Football Scout →

Leeds United

Gabriel Gudmundsson (£3.8m) has been ruled out of Gameweek 35 after suffering a hamstring injury at Wembley last weekend. The Swedish international will also likely miss Gameweek 36 against Tottenham.

Ilia Gruev (£4.7m) is out for the remainder of the season with a hamstring injury.

Noah Okafor (£5.6m) is a doubt for Friday's fixture after picking up a calf issue. His availability will be monitored.

Jaka Bijol (£3.9m) is a concern heading into the game, having been dealing with a hip flexor problem.

Anton Stach (£4.8m) returned from an ankle injury as a substitute in Sunday's FA Cup semi-final and could feature on Friday.

James Justin (£3.9m) is the favourite to deputise for Gudmundsson at wing-back.

Aston Villa

Boubacar Kamara (£4.9m) remains a long-term absentee with a knee injury.

Amadou Onana (£4.8m) faces assessment ahead of Thursday's clash with Nottingham Forest, having missed Saturday's defeat at Fulham due to pain.

Ross Barkley and Alysson were ineligible for Thursday's game as they were not named in Villa's Europa League squad, but both are expected to return for Gameweek 35.
