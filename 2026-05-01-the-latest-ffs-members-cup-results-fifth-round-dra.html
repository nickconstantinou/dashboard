FFS Members Cup: Round Four Results and Fifth-Round Draw

Overview

The Fantasy Football Scout Members Cup has reached its penultimate stages. Sixteen FPL managers remain in contention following the completion of Round Four, with Round Five set to take place in Gameweek 35.

Round Four Results

The fourth round delivered several notable upsets and high-scoring performances. Manshizzle (1.19m) and Hawk Eye (542k) emerged as the joint top scorers of Gameweek 34, both recording an impressive 76 points.

Despite these strong performances, the round proved costly for several high-profile managers. Fellow top 10k manager Johnny 8 was eliminated, losing to Team Dependant (597k). Manshizzle's opponent, FPLJabroni, was unlucky to be knocked out on a score of 68 points. Similarly, Chemical76 was eliminated with 69 points.

The headline news saw the highest-ranked participant, Berries, suffer elimination. Berries, who was previously ranked 847th before falling to 934th, lost narrowly by 58 points to 60 against Off the Clough (809k).

In the tie-draw between UpminsterTrainman (106k) and JoleTouriste (274k), UpminsterTrainman progressed because of his higher worldwide position. This highlights how tie-draw situations in the Members Cup can be decided by overall rankings when scores are equal.

Performance of Top Managers

The latest round proved particularly challenging for elite managers. In fact, just one of the best 10 managers to enter Gameweek 34 was successful. That manager was DaveZubie (12k), who managed to survive and progress through to Round Five.

Fifth-Round Preview

Round Five will take place in Gameweek 35, where the 16 remaining managers will compete for the coveted prizes. Nobody else in the fifth round is currently inside the top 75k, making this a particularly competitive field of mid-tier managers.

Prize Structure

The Members Cup offers the following prizes for the top three finishers:

  • 1st Place: £150 Amazon voucher
  • 2nd Place: £100 Amazon voucher
  • 3rd Place: £50 Amazon voucher
