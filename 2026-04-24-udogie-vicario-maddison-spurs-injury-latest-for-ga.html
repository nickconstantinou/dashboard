Tottenham Injury Report: Gameweek 34 Availability

Defensive Concerns

Destiny Udogie is likely to miss Gameweek 34 with the muscle injury he suffered last weekend. Manager Roberto De Zerbi has expressed hope that Udogie can be available for the next game at Villa Park, but he will not feature this weekend. Udogie is priced at £4.3m.

Guglielmo Vicario underwent hernia surgery and is not ready to return. The goalkeeper remains unavailable and is priced at £4.7m.

Additional Defenders Sidelined

Several other defenders continue to miss action. Cristian Romero is sidelined with a knee injury and is priced at £5.0m. Ben Davies is sidelined with an ankle injury and is priced at £4.3m.

Midfield and Attack Absences

Dejan Kulusevski is sidelined with a knee injury and is priced at £6.4m. Mohammed Kudus is sidelined with a thigh injury and is priced at £6.4m. Wilson Odobert is sidelined with a knee injury and is priced at £5.3m.

Maddison Remains Unavailable But on Bench

James Maddison was a surprise inclusion on the Spurs bench last weekend but did not make it onto the field. He experienced pain this week and is not available to play, but he will be on the bench for Gameweek 34. Maddison is priced at £6.8m.
