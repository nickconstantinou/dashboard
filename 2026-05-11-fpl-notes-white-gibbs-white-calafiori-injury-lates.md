# FPL Notes: White Injury, Calafiori Update, and Selection Uncertainties

## Ben White Injury Concern

Ben White (£5.1m) suffered a concerning injury during the recent fixture. The defender hobbled off the pitch with a knee injury and departed the ground wearing a leg brace. While no definitive prognosis has been established given the early stage of assessment, this development could potentially mark the end of his season.

## Riccardo Calafiori Also a Doubt

Riccardo Calafiori (£5.6m) was another Arsenal defender who went off at half-time during the same match. His issue remains undisclosed at this stage. Fantasy managers will need to monitor both defenders' statuses ahead of the upcoming fixture.

## Arsenal Fixture Outlook

Arsenal's final home match of the season presents itself against relegated Burnley in Gameweek 37 on Monday. Whether Calafiori will be available for this encounter remains uncertain following his half-time withdrawal.

Eberechi Eze (£7.3m) faces potential removal from Arsenal's starting eleven. With Martin Odegaard (£7.8m) having produced his seventh assist of the campaign during the match, the Norwegian midfielder may have played his way back into contention for the Gunners' final home fixture. This development places Eze's position at risk within the starting lineup.
