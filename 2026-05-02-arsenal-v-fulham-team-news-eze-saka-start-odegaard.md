# Arsenal v Fulham: Saka and Eze Return as Arteta Makes Five Changes

## Saka and Eze Back in the Starting XI

Bukayo Saka returns to the Arsenal starting XI this evening after recovering from injury. The England international, priced at £9.8m, was declared fit again and lines up from the kickoff against Fulham at the Emirates Stadium. Eberechi Eze, priced at £7.3m, also returns to the starting lineup alongside Saka.

## Arteta Rings the Changes

Mikel Arteta has made five changes from the midweek draw in Madrid. The Arsenal manager has shuffled his pack significantly for this Premier League encounter, bringing several players back into the fold after various rest periods.

## Further Incoming Changes

Leandro Trossard, available at £6.5m, is brought back into the starting XI as part of the defensive reshuffle. In midfield, Myles Lewis-Skelly, priced at £5.0m, comes into the side as Martin Zubimendi drops to the bench. At left-back, Riccardo Calafiori replaces Piero Hincapie, with Calafiori priced at £5.6m for Fantasy purposes.

## Benchwarmers to Monitor

Noni Madueke, valued at £6.8m, and Gabriel Martinelli have to make do with substitute duty this evening. Fantasy managers who have invested in either player will need to look elsewhere for returns this gameweek.

## Fulham Team News

Harrison Reed comes into Fulham's starting line-up for this match. Reed is available at £4.3m. Harrison Reed and Antonee Robinson come into the line-up as the visitors look to compete at the Emirates Stadium.

## Absent Players

Sander Berge and Ryan Sessegnon are unavailable for Fulham through illness and injury respectively. Berge is priced at £4.9m but will not feature. For Arsenal, Martin Odegaard is absent entirely from the squad. Arteta had raised doubts about the Norwegian midfielder's availability on Friday before confirmation came that he would not be involved.
