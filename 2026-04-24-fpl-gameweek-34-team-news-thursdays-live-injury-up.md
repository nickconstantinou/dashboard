# FPL Gameweek 34 Team News: Thursday's Live Injury Updates

## Sunderland

Sunderland's defensive options have been hit by injuries heading into Gameweek 34. Omar Alderete is available for selection despite coming off at Villa Park last week. The defender is priced at £4.1m and could feature this gameweek.

However, there is concerning news regarding Romaine Mundle. The midfielder is likely out for the season after suffering a hamstring injury. He carries a price tag of £4.9m, making him a significant absence for managers.

Third-choice goalkeeper Simon Moore has also joined the injury list after sustaining a hand injury. He is priced at £3.9m.

## Nottingham Forest

Nottingham Forest have mixed injury news ahead of this fixture. Dan Ndoye is in contention to return to action. The club confirmed he will be in condition to play the game, which is positive news for Forest and FPL managers alike. Ndoye is priced at £5.5m.

In less encouraging news, Callum Hudson-Odoi underwent surgery and is currently recovering. The forward is priced at £5.7m. Forest manager Nuno Espírito Santo commented that he is okay after surgery and is an important player, but the priority is for him to fully recover.

## Manchester United

Leny Yoro is hoping to feature for Manchester United after picking up a knock in training last week. The defender is available at £4.1m and could be in contention for selection this gameweek.

## Additional Squad Updates

Dan Ballard is in contention to start after returning from injury as a substitute in Gameweek 33. He could feature in Sunderland's plans this fixture.

Nilson Angulo and Bertrand Traore should return to training next week as they continue their recovery.

Jocelin Ta Bi has started sessions this week and may go further next week after feeling good during rehabilitation.

Nottingham Forest defender Murillo has been ruled out of the trip to the Stadium of Light after suffering a muscle injury in Gameweek 33.
