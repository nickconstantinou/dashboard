# Fantasy EFL Play-Offs is LIVE!

The Fantasy EFL Play-Offs is now available as a separate event from the regular Fantasy EFL game. This competition runs independently, meaning every participant starts with zero points regardless of their performance in the main game.

## Event Structure

The Play-Offs spans three Gameweeks, mirroring the football season's play-off format. Gameweek 1 covers the semi-final first legs, Gameweek 2 features the second legs, and Gameweek 3 determines the champion in the final.

## Selection Rules

Managers must select exactly 7 players in one of the valid formations. There is a club limit of 2 clubs for Gameweeks 1 and 2, increasing to 3 clubs in Gameweek 3. Each manager must also designate 1 captain and 1 vice-captain for their squad.

One key advantage is that you can view the teamsheets before finalising your selections, allowing for more informed decisions.

## Free Hit Every Gameweek

Each Gameweek functions as a virtual Free Hit, giving managers complete flexibility to reset their squad without consequence. This applies across all three Gameweeks.

## Prizes

**Gameweek 1 (Semi-Final)**
First place receives 2x tickets to a 2025/26 Play-Off Final of choice, along with a branded Fantasy EFL merchandise pack.

**Gameweek 2 (Semi-Final)**
First place earns a replica shirt or EFL football signed by players from a club participating in the 2025/26 EFL Play-Offs, plus branded merchandise.

**Gameweek 3 (Final)**
First place wins the same signed item prize package as Gameweek 2.

**Overall Winner**
The champion receives 2x hospitality tickets to a 2026/27 EFL Play-Off Final of their choice, a signed replica shirt or football, and branded merchandise.
