# Man City v Crystal Palace: Team News and Lineups Confirmed

**Match Overview**

The Premier League fixture between Manchester City and Crystal Palace is set to kick off at 20:00 BST at the Etihad Stadium. Both managers have made significant changes to their starting lineups for this encounter.

## Manchester City Team News

Pep Guardiola has made six changes to the Manchester City side that beat Brentford on Saturday. Several prominent players have been moved to substitute duty. Well-owned FPL picks Erling Haaland, Rayan Cherki, and Jeremy Doku are among the players dropping to the bench. Nathan Ake and Tijjani Reijnders also move down to substitute duty.

On a positive note, Josko Gvardiol returns to the starting XI for the first time since early January, providing a boost to the City defense.

Rodri remains unavailable for selection, as the Spanish midfielder continues his recovery on the sidelines.

Nico O'Reilly is not included in the matchday squad at all. The midfielder, priced at £5.3m, has been left out entirely for this fixture.

## Crystal Palace Team News

Crystal Palace manager Oliver Glasner has also tinkered with his selection, making four changes from the draw with Everton. Jean Philippe-Mateta, Yeremy Pino, Will Hughes, and Jefferson Lerma all return to the starting XI for this match.

Conversely, Jorgen Strand Larsen, Ismaila Sarr, Daichi Kamada, and Adam Wharton drop to the bench for the Eagles.

## Key Fantasy Implications

For Fantasy Premier League managers, several high-profile assets find themselves on the bench for this match. Haaland, Cherki, and Doku's rotation means their captaincy potential is reduced for this gameweek. Meanwhile, O'Reilly's omission from the squad eliminates him as a budget enabler option entirely.
