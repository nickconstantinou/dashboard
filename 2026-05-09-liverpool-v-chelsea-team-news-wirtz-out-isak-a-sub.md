# Liverpool v Chelsea Team News

## Key Liverpool Absence

Florian Wirtz will miss Liverpool's match against Chelsea after being ruled out with a stomach bug. The midfielder, priced at £8.3m, was unavailable for selection due to the illness. His absence represents an enforced change to the Liverpool lineup for this fixture.

## Replacement Starter

Rio Ngumoha comes into the starting XI to replace Wirtz. The young player, valued at £4.3m, gets the nod to start in place of the ill Wirtz.

## Isak Returns to Bench

Alexander Isak is back on the bench for Liverpool after a brief absence. The striker, priced at £10.3m, returns to the squad for this encounter against Chelsea.

## Goalkeeper Change

Giorgi Mamardashvili returns in goal for Liverpool after recovering from injury. The goalkeeper, valued at £4.1m, displaces Freddie Woodman in the starting lineup. Mamardashvili's return strengthens the Liverpool defence following his recovery from injury.

## Left-Back Switch

Milos Kerkez replaces Andrew Robertson at left-back for Liverpool. The defender, priced at £5.6m, comes into the side for this fixture. This represents one of the expected changes to the Liverpool lineup.

## Ongoing Absences

Mohamed Salah and Alisson remain out for Liverpool but are close to returning to training. Both players, with Salah priced at £14.0m, are described as "close" to a return to training and could be available soon, though they miss this match against Chelsea.

## Three Changes Made

Arne Slot makes three changes from the Liverpool side that lost to Manchester United last weekend. The enforced absence of Wirtz, combined with the returns of Mamardashvili and Kerkez, accounts for these alterations to the starting lineup.

## Chelsea Lineup Changes

Levi Colwill makes his first start of the season for Chelsea. Jorrel Hato and Wesley Fofana also come in to a new-look Chelsea defence for this match, suggesting significant changes to the visiting side's backline.

## Chelsea Substitutions

Reece James has to make do with substitute duty again for Chelsea. The defender, priced at £5.6m, starts on the bench despite being available for selection.

## Chelsea Injuries

Robert Sanchez and teenage attacker Jesse Derry miss Chelsea's match with head injuries. Both players were unavailable for the trip to Liverpool due to these issues.
