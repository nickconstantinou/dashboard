The Fantasy Football Scout (FFS) Members Cup continues to deliver dramatic moments as round five approaches. With 16 managers remaining, the competition intensifies heading into Gameweek 35.

Notable Results and High-Profile Casualties

The headline news from round four saw the highest-ranked participant, Berries (previously 847th, now 934th), exit the competition after a narrow 58-60 defeat to Off the Clough (ranked 809k). Another notable casualty was fellow top 10k manager Johnny 8, who lost to Team Dependant (597k). These results highlight the unpredictable nature of cup competitions even for experienced managers.

Top Scorers of Gameweek 34

Their performances secured progress to the next stage.

Narrow Margins and Heartbreak

Several managers suffered painful exits despite respectable scores. FPLJabroni was unlucky to be eliminated with 68 points, while Chemical76 also departed after scoring 69 points. These tight margins underscore how fantasy football fortunes can turn on individual matchups.

Among top entrants, DaveZubie (ranked 12k) proved to be just one of the best 10 managers to successfully navigate Gameweek 34, standing out as the sole successful entrant from the elite tier.

Tiebreaker Decision

One match required a tiebreaker resolution. UpminsterTrainman (106k) won a 50-50 draw with JoleTouriste (274k) because of his higher worldwide position, allowing him to progress despite the tied score.

Prize Structure

The competition offers attractive prizes for the remaining participants: first place receives a £150 Amazon voucher, second place earns a £100 Amazon voucher, and third place secures a £50 Amazon voucher.
