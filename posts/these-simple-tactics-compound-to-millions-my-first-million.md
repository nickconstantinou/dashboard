# These Simple Tactics Compound to Millions

## Introduction

In the world of startups and investing, there are certain tactics that, when applied consistently over time, can lead to massive wealth creation. These aren't get-rich-quick schemes - they're proven strategies that compound.

## The Power of Compound Thinking

The most successful investors and entrepreneurs think in terms of compounding returns. Instead of looking for quick wins, they focus on systems that grow exponentially over time.

## Key Tactics

### 1. Reinvest Everything

Every dollar you earn should be put to work. Dividend reinvestment, profit reinvestment, skill reinvestment - the compound effect works in all areas.

### 2. Build Assets, Not Liabilities

An asset puts money in your pocket. A liability takes money out. Focus on building assets that appreciate over time.

### 3. Think in Decades, Not Days

The people who become wealthy think in long time horizons. A $10,000 investment at 7% becomes $76,000 in 30 years - without adding a single additional dollar.

## Conclusion

These tactics aren't sexy. They don't make for exciting social media posts. But they work. Consistently. Over time.
