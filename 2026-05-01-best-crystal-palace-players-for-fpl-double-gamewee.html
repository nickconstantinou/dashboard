Best Crystal Palace Players for FPL Double Gameweek 36

Double Gameweek Overview

Crystal Palace will double in Gameweek 36, with matches against Everton and Manchester City. This is the final Double Gameweek of the 2025/26 season.

Crystal Palace have not had any doubles to date in 2025/26, making this a notable opportunity for FPL managers. The team blanked in Gameweek 31, which may have influenced some managers to look elsewhere, but the upcoming double fixture presents a chance to capitalise on Palace coverage.

Fixture Breakdown

Everton (Away)

Crystal Palace travel to Goodison Park for their first fixture of this Double Gameweek. Everton rank 12th out of 15 teams for xG/xGC on the road, suggesting their away defence has been below average by underlying metrics.

Additionally, Everton are without a clean sheet in their last six away matches. However, it is worth noting that Everton have not shipped more than two goals in any away fixture in 2025/26, indicating they can be leaky defensively without being completely breached.

Manchester City (Home)

The second fixture sees Crystal Palace host Manchester City. City, who host Palace in Gameweek 36, are 2nd or 3rd for xG/xGC in home fixtures in 2025/26, highlighting their strong home underlying numbers.

City have kept a fourth-best 11 clean sheets this season. They also haven't lost at home in the league since August, making them a formidable opponent on their own ground.

Tactical Considerations

The contrasting nature of these fixtures presents a mixed outlook for Palace assets. The away game at Everton appears more favourable based on Everton's poor away xG/xGC ranking and lack of clean sheets on the road. The home game against Manchester City looks challenging given City rank among the top teams for home underlying metrics and have been unbeaten at home since August.

Managers weighing up Crystal Palace options for Double Gameweek 36 should consider whether the two fixtures cancel each other out or whether the more favourable Everton fixture tips the balance.
