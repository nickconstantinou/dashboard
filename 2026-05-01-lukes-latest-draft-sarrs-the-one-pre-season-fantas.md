# Luke's Latest Draft: FPL Goalkeeper Strategy for 2025/26

Luke from FPL Blackbox has shared his current draft strategy for the 2025/26 Fantasy Premier League season, offering insights into his goalkeeper selection process. For the past three seasons, Luke has consistently used Rya as his 4.5 million goalkeeper. ## The Risk of Newly Signed Players

Luke strongly advises avoiding newly signed players at the start of the season. His reasoning is practical: recent arrivals may drop in value initially. Managers typically need time to integrate new signings into their systems, often requiring several days of training before they are ready for regular selection. This adjustment period can translate to delayed starts and potential price reductions for FPL managers who pick these players early.

## Key Takeaways
