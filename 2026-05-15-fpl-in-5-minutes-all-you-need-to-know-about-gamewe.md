# FPL in 5 minutes: All you need to know about Gameweek 37

## Deadline and Matchday Structure

All transfers and team selections need to be completed by 18:30 BST on Friday 15 May. An important scheduling note for this gameweek is that no top-flight matches are happening on Saturday in Gameweek 37.

## Standout Fixture

Arsenal vs Burnley is Gameweek 37's clear standout fixture. Arsenal will go five points ahead if they beat already-relegated Burnley at home, making this a potentially pivotal match in the title race.

## Recommended Players

Bukayo Saka (£10.0m) and Viktor Gyokeres (£9.0m) are unanimous Scout Squad picks. Leandro Trossard (£6.5m) is an endorsed mid-priced replacement for Morgan Rogers (£7.4m) and Morgan Gibbs-White (£7.5m). It's worth noting that most of Viktor Gyokeres' goals come against struggling sides.

## Team News and Rotation Notes

Recent team selection patterns are worth monitoring. The rotation in Gameweek 36 was done with the FA Cup final against Chelsea in mind. Pep Guardiola benched Rayan Cherki (£6.6m) and Jeremy Doku (£6.5m) in Double Gameweek 36. Additionally, Guardiola did not use Erling Haaland (£14.7m) or Nico O'Reilly (£5.3m) at all in Double Gameweek 36, suggesting possible rest or rotation strategies for key players.
