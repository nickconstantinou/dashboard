Man City Focus: The Nico O'Reilly Proposition

Nico O'Reilly has delivered four goals and one assist in his last seven matches. His underlying numbers reinforce the attacking intent driving this output. O'Reilly has had six shots from inside the opposition's six-yard box in his last seven matches. At £5.2m, he offers Man City coverage at a mid-price point that leaves budget for premium assets elsewhere.

Arsenal Fixture Analysis

Arsenal entertain a Fulham side that has scored only 16 away goals all season. This away scoring record provides a favorable backdrop for Arsenal's defensive options. Fulham have failed to score in four of their last five away matches. The absence of key creative players compounds Fulham's attacking difficulties, with Fulham also devoid of the creativity of Alex Iwobi (£6.2m).

Arsenal Defensive Asset

Gabriel Magalhaes has eight attacking returns this season, the fourth-most of any defender. This haul places him among the most productive defenders for FPL purposes when looking for offensive contributions from the backline.

Bournemouth Budget Option

Bournemouth have kept five clean sheets on home turf this season, all against teams that are currently ranked eighth or below in the table. James Hill is joint-top among all defenders for DefCon points across the last six matches with 10 points. At £4.2m, he represents a budget-friendly route into a defence that has shown competence against lower-ranked opponents. His recent DefCon consistency makes him a viable option for managers seeking value in defence.

Rotation Concerns

Crystal Palace is one of the division's lowest scorers who could rotate in Gameweek 35. Managers considering Palace assets should factor in potential lineup changes.

Key Takeaways for Gameweek 35

Man City and Arsenal anchor the recommended picks, with Fulham's away struggles supporting both teams' clean sheet prospects. Among budget options, James Hill at £4.2m offers a statistically backed route into Bournemouth's defence, while Nico O'Reilly at £5.2m provides an affordable entry point to Man City's attacking rotation.
