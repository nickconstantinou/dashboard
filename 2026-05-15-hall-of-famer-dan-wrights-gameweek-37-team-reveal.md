# Dan Wright's Gameweek 37 Team Reveal

## Background

Dan Wright has built an impressive fantasy football record with five top 10k finishes, which means he ranks 17th in the Career Hall of Fame. However, recent Gameweeks have presented challenges.

## Current Situation

With no chips left to play, a red arrow always looked likely in Gameweek 36. This performance dropped him down to an overall rank of 50k. He heads into Gameweek 37 with just one free transfer.

## Tactical Approach

Dan Wright is currently managing six Arsenal and Manchester City players. With only two Gameweeks remaining, he believes it is important to stay aggressive with transfers. Because of that, he seriously considered rolling the free transfer this week but ultimately decided to use it.

## Key Player Mention

Antoine Semenyo (£8.1m) scored against Crystal Palace and is part of the Man City squad.

## Looking Ahead

Rotation in Gameweek 38 is also a growing concern, especially if the title race is wrapped up by then. Dan Wright's strategy focuses on maximizing his remaining transfers in the final stretch of the season.
