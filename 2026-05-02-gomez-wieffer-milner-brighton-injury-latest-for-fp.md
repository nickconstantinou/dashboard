# Brighton Injury Latest for FPL Gameweek 35

## Overview

Brighton & Hove Albion head into Gameweek 35 with several squad changes affecting Fantasy Premier League managers. Here is the latest on the key players ahead of the fixture.

## Attacking Options Returning

Danny Welbeck comes back into reckoning for Gameweek 35. The forward had his usual midweek benching in Brighton's last Premier League match but returns to consideration for the upcoming fixture. Welbeck's price stands at £6.3m, and he notably scored two goals against Newcastle in the reverse fixture.

## Midfield Updates

James Milner has an unspecified issue and won't return from the last three games. The midfielder remains unavailable at a price of £4.9m. Solly March is still sidelined with a muscle problem at his price of £5.8m. Regarding these two players, manager Fabian Hurzeler stated: "Unfortunately, both not available, but both very close. They will be in training next week."

Mats Wieffer has an ongoing ankle niggle that forced him off against Chelsea at half-time. However, the midfielder trained all week and is available for Gameweek 35 at £4.9m. The manager confirmed: "He trained the whole week... he will be available for tomorrow."

## Longer-Term Absentees

Diego Gomez (knee) is unavailable for Gameweek 35 but manager Fabian Hurzeler expects him back this season. The manager stated: "We'll see him back this season. I think he's also quite close to coming back to the training sessions." Hurzeler also indicated: "He's back on the training pitch and he will be available, I think, next week."

Stefanos Tzimas and Adam Webster are long-term absentees with knee injuries.

## FPL Considerations

For Gameweek 35, Brighton's most relevant options appear to be Danny Welbeck at £6.3m and Mats Wieffer at £4.9m, with Welbeck having demonstrated his scoring threat against Newcastle this season. Managers should monitor the returns of Milner and March next week, while Gomez represents a longer-term investment for the remainder of the season.
