Mitoma, Gomez, Wieffer: Brighton Injury Latest for FPL Gameweek 37

Key Injury Updates

Kaoru Mitoma (Source Only)

Kaoru Mitoma will miss Brighton's final two matches due to a hamstring injury. He picked up the injury last Saturday and will be unavailable for selection.

Diego Gomez (Source Only)

Diego Gomez is available for selection after recovering from a knee injury.

Mats Wieffer - £4.9m

Mats Wieffer could return for Sunday's match. The midfielder is priced at £4.9m and there is an option that he will be available on Sunday, though the club must wait the next two to three days to confirm his participation.

Long-Term Absences

Stefanos Tzimas - £5.2m

Stefanos Tzimas remains on the longer-term injury list with a knee injury. He is valued at £5.2m in FPL.

Adam Webster - £4.4m

Adam Webster also remains on the longer-term injury list with a knee injury. The defender is priced at £4.4m.

Tactical Notes and Opportunities

Jack Hinshelwood - £5.1m

Jack Hinshelwood is priced at £5.1m and is aiming to become the first Brighton player to score in four successive Premier League matches. His recent scoring streak presents an interesting option for FPL managers considering Brighton's fixtures.

Maxim De Cuyper - £4.3m

Maxim De Cuyper will start at Leeds United after providing two assists last weekend. Fabian Hurzeler hinted that De Cuyper will again get the nod for this fixture. At £4.3m, he offers budget-friendly attacking potential from defence.

FPL Considerations

For Gameweek 37, Brighton managers should note that Mitoma's absence leaves a gap in Brighton's attacking options, while Gomez's availability provides flexibility. Hinshelwood's scoring streak makes him a standout option at £5.1m, whereas De Cuyper represents a budget route into Brighton's attack at £4.3m. Tzimas and Webster remain unavailable for the foreseeable future.
