# FPL Gameweek 37 Team News: Friday's Live Injury Updates

## Newcastle United: Lewis Hall Fit, Joelinton Doubt

Newcastle United attacker Lewis Hall has returned to full training and is available for selection ahead of Gameweek 37. The £5.3m defender required treatment following the club's previous match, but has trained throughout the week and is ready for inclusion in Fantasy Premier League squads.

However, manager Eddie Howe faces a fresh concern over midfielder Joelinton, who has emerged as a doubt due to a thigh problem. This new injury concern comes at an unwelcome time for managers considering Newcastle assets.

Several players remain unavailable for selection. Lewis Miley (foot), Emil Krafth (knee), Tino Livramento (thigh), and Fabian Schar (ankle/foot) are all still sidelined and will not feature this gameweek.
