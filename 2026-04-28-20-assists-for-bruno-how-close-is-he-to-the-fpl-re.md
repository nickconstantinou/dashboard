# Bruno Fernandes Reaches 20 FPL Assists: How Close Is He to the Record?

## A Record-Breaking Season

Bruno Fernandes has reached 20 FPL assists in the 2025/26 season, cementing his status as the most prolific assist-provider in Fantasy Premier League this campaign. This achievement places him eight assists ahead of any other player, highlighting a remarkable gap between him and his closest competitors. His total FPL points for the season stand at 204, demonstrating the substantial fantasy value he has provided to managers who selected him.

## The 20-Assist Benchmark

The orthodox FPL assists record of 20 has been co-held by two legendary figures: Thierry Henry, who achieved this feat in the 2002/03 season, and Kevin De Bruyne, who matched it in the 2019/20 campaign. Reaching this milestone after Gameweek 34 means Fernandes is just one assist away from matching this historic benchmark. His performance places him joint-fifth in the all-time FPL assists table, reflecting his consistent ability to create opportunities for his teammates throughout the season.

## The True Record Holder

While 20 assists represents the traditional record, the actual single-season FPL assists record belongs to Luis Suarez, who registered 25 assists during the 2013/14 season. Remarkably, Suarez achieved this extraordinary tally despite missing the first five matches of that campaign through suspension. Beyond his assist total, Suarez also holds the all-time FPL record for total attacking returns in a single campaign, having registered 55 across goals and assists during that remarkable season.

## A Unique Assist

Among Bruno Fernandes's 20 assists this season, his only 'Fantasy assist'—defined as a pass leading directly to an opponent's own goal—was recorded for James Hill's own-goal in Gameweek 31. This distinction highlights how the vast majority of his creative contributions have directly resulted in his teammates finding the net.

## Chasing History

With one match remaining to potentially add to his tally, Bruno Fernandes stands at the threshold of equalling the orthodox 20-assist record held by Henry and De Bruyne. His dominance this season—measured not only by assists but by an eight-assist margin over his nearest rival and a tally of 204 total points—positions him among the all-time greats in FPL history. Whether he surpasses the 20-mark or falls just short, his 2025/26 campaign represents one of the most impressive creative performances the game has witnessed.
