# Everton Injury Latest for Gameweek 34: Beto, Branthwaite, Grealish

## Overview

Everton manager David Moyes has confirmed the club's current injury situation heading into Gameweek 34, with two players confirmed as unavailable for the upcoming fixtures.

## Beto

Beto will miss Everton's game against West Ham United due to concussion. The forward sustained a head injury and is required to follow concussion protocol before returning to action. His absence leaves a gap in Everton's attacking options for this fixture.

## Jarrad Branthwaite

Jarrad Branthwaite faces a season-ending absence due to a hamstring injury. Manager David Moyes stated definitively: "Jarrad will be out for the season... he won't be fit again to play this season." Earlier in the week, the club had indicated the defender was "unlikely" to feature again this campaign, and this has now been confirmed as a complete season-ending situation. Branthwaite is priced at £5.3m in Fantasy Premier League.

## Jack Grealish

Jack Grealish remains a long-term absentee for Everton following a foot injury. The midfielder, priced at £6.3m, has been unavailable for an extended period and continues his recovery.

## Current Squad Status

According to David Moyes, Beto and Branthwaite are currently the only two players sidelined, aside from the long-term absence of Grealish. This gives Fantasy managers a clear picture of Everton's injury situation heading into Gameweek 34.
