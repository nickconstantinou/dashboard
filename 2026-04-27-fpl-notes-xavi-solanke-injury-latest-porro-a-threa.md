# FPL Notes: Xavi Simons Injury Update, Porro Emerges as Threat

## Injury Situation at Tottenham

Xavi Simons (£6.6m) suffered a significant setback during the recent match, departing on a stretcher with a knee injury. The assessment will take place Monday or Tuesday, leaving Fantasy managers in a difficult position. The Spurs attacking midfielder had been a popular target ahead of Gameweek 34, with combined transfer activity for both Xavi Simons and Dominic Solanke exceeding 1.1 million moves.

Dominic Solanke also hobbled off in the first half with what appeared to be a hamstring concern. Manager Roberto De Zerbi provided some reassurance regarding Solanke's condition, stating it "is not a big problem." However, the situation differs markedly for Xavi Simons, whose knee injury presents greater concern.

## Potential Replacements

With Xavi Simons facing assessment and potential sidelining, Mathys Tel (£6.2m) emerges as the primary candidate to deputise. The young forward has shown promising signs when introduced from the bench and could represent a budget-friendly option for managers seeking to replace Simons' attacking output.

Additionally, Richarlison stands to benefit from Solanke's potential absence, offering another forward option for Fantasy managers to consider as they reassess their squads.

## Pedro Porro Delivers Defensive Value

Despite limited attacking returns, Pedro Porro delivered solid Fantasy returns in the match against Wolves. The Spurs defender contributed through a clean sheet, DefCon points, and two bonus points. Porro came close to an assist on multiple occasions, suggesting underlying attacking potential despite the modest official returns.

## Match Context

Tottenham secured their first league win and clean sheet of 2026 at Molineux against Wolves. Joao Palhinha (£5.5m) proved decisive, scoring the opening goal in the 82nd minute. The strike accounted for well over half of Spurs' total xG (1.05), highlighting his importance to the team during this fixture. Wolves remain relegated despite an improved performance that saw them compete competitively throughout the match.
