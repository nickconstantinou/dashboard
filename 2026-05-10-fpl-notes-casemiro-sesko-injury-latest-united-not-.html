FPL notes: Casemiro + Sesko injury latest, United not ‘on the beach’

Injury Updates

Benjamin Sesko

Benjamin Sesko (£7.3m) continues to be troubled by shin issues. The forward remains plagued by these shin problems, which have affected his availability and consistency throughout the season. Managers considering the player should factor in this recurring issue when evaluating their FPL squads.

Casemiro

Casemiro (£5.9m) was absent from Manchester United's recent fixture due to a shin problem. The Brazilian midfielder made no appearance in the match, despite being in strong form prior to the injury. However, Michael Carrick has indicated that Casemiro should be fine for Gameweek 37, describing the issue as "nothing major." Casemiro has been in excellent form recently, recording just one blank in his last nine matches when counting the DefCon goal as a return.

Manuel Ugarte

Manuel Ugarte (£4.7m) was also missing from the United squad, sitting out the match with a back problem.

Gameweek 37 Preview

The upcoming Gameweek 37 holds special significance for Casemiro, as it marks his farewell at Old Trafford. Managers holding the midfielder may see this as a potential emotional boost or factor in rotation risks, though Carrick's update suggests he should feature.

Tactical Notes: Manchester United

Manchester United's attacking performance raised concerns, with the team waiting until the 95th minute to register their first shot on target. This sluggish start contrasts with their recent positive results and may be worth monitoring as the season reaches its conclusion.

Notable Performances

Sunderland's Defensive Record

Sunderland continue to impress at the back, accumulating 11 clean sheets for the season. This defensive solidity makes their assets attractive for managers seeking budget options in defence.

Senne Lammens

Senne Lammens (£5.1m) demonstrated his value between the posts, making several smart saves during the match. The goalkeeper kept out efforts from Noah Sadaki (£4.9m) and Brian Brobbey (£5.3m), showcasing his importance to his side's defensive efforts.

Bench Observations

Bryan Mbuemo (£8.3m) was among the notable players who started the match on the bench, suggesting rotation risks for premium attackers as clubs manage their squads toward the season's end.
