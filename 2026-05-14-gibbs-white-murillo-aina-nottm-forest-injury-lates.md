# Nottingham Forest Injury News for FPL Gameweek 37

Nottingham Forest face Manchester United in Fantasy Premier League Gameweek 37, and manager Vitor Pereira has provided updates on several key players ahead of the fixture.

## Morgan Gibbs-White Returns to Training

Morgan Gibbs-White looks set to feature in Gameweek 37 after returning to training wearing a protective mask. The midfielder, priced at £7.5m, was photographed training with the team, and Pereira confirmed the player has adapted well to the mask. Pereira stated: "He started today to work with the team, with the mask. Let's see. It is strange when you use the mask but, in the end, I spoke with him and he said, 'I'm okay with the mask, no problem'." His return would be a significant boost for Forest ahead of the crucial fixture against Manchester United.

## Dan Ndoye Nearing Return

Dan Ndoye, valued at £5.5m, could return from a short-term issue ahead of Gameweek 37. Pereira indicated: "Dan Ndoye started [training]." The winger's return would provide additional attacking options for the Midlands club as they push for a strong finish to the season.

## Sangare Could Feature

Ibrahima Sangare may also return to action after missing recent matches. Reports indicate: "I think Sangare [will train] tomorrow." Sangare's potential return would bolster Forest's midfield options, though any involvement would likely depend on how training progresses this week.

## Ola Aina Remains Unavailable

Ola Aina remains out with an unspecified injury. The defender, priced at £4.7m, continues to be unavailable for selection, leaving Forest without a key defensive option for the trip to Manchester United. Fantasy managers should not expect Aina to feature this gameweek.

## Murillo Remains Sidelined

Murillo continues to recover from a muscle injury and remains unavailable. Pereira explained: "Murillo out, Aina out." Furthermore, the manager expressed uncertainty about whether Murillo and Sangare will feature again this season, stating: "I really don't know [whether Murillo and Sangare will feature again this season]. They are improving but I don't know. I think it is not a time for risks." This raises concerns about both players' availability for the remainder of the campaign.

## FPL Considerations

For Gameweek 37, Gibbs-White represents the most promising Forest asset if selected, particularly given his £7.5m price point and potential involvement against Manchester United. Ndoye at £5.5m could emerge as a budget option if he returns. However, managers should avoid Aina and Murillo this gameweek, as both remain confirmed absences. Sangare remains a gamble given the manager's uncertainty about his season involvement.
