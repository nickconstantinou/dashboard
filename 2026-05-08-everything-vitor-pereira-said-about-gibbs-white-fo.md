# Vitor Pereira Provides Injury Updates on Gibbs-White and Forest Players

## Morgan Gibbs-White's Facial Injury

Morgan Gibbs-White of Nottingham Forest suffered a facial injury on Monday, according to Vitor Pereira. Despite the injury, Gibbs-White was among the substitutes for selection. When asked whether the player had any chance of playing on Thursday, Pereira responded definitively: "No, [there was no chance of him playing today]."

At his price point of £7.7m, Gibbs-White's facial injury has rendered him unavailable for Forest's recent fixtures. His status remains uncertain pending further assessment.

## Callum Hudson-Odoi: Season-Ending Injury

Pereira confirmed that Callum Hudson-Odoi is finished for the season. The England-priced player, valued at £5.7m, will not feature again this campaign. This marks a significant setback for Forest's attacking options heading into the final stretch of the season.

## Dan Ndoye's Potential Return

Regarding Dan Ndoye, Pereira expressed cautious optimism about a possible return to action. The manager stated: "Dan, I think Dan maybe can come back." However, his exact timeline remains unclear, and his availability for upcoming fixtures is yet to be confirmed.

## Murillo's Recovery from Muscle Injury

Murillo had been out for a fortnight with a muscle injury prior to Forest's recent match. The Brazilian defender successfully returned to action, coming on in the 88th minute for a cramping Jair Cunha. This substitute appearance indicated his return to match fitness, though he had been managing the muscle injury in the preceding weeks.

## Pereira's Assessment of Player Availability

Pereira addressed the broader squad situation by confirming that several key players were not in condition to play. He listed Gibbs-White, Sangare, Dan Ndoye, and Murillo as not meeting the conditions required for selection. Only Lucca, Bakwa, and Yates were brought as players in conditions to play during the match in question.

When pressed on recovery prospects for the next match, Pereira expressed uncertainty: "I don't know if some or one of them can recover for the next match. I don't know." The manager indicated he would assess the situation on Friday to understand player availability more clearly: "But in the end, I need tomorrow. That's the day to understand the situation."

## Summary of Current Injury Situation

Forest face significant injury concerns heading into their next fixture. Gibbs-White remains unavailable due to his facial injury, while Hudson-Odoi is ruled out for the remainder of the season. Sangare also remains unavailable. Ndoye's potential return offers some hope, though Murillo has now returned to action after his muscle injury. Pereira is expected to provide a clearer picture of player availability following Friday's assessment.
