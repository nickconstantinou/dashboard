# FPL Rate My Team Surgery with Tom Freeman

## An Expert Resource for Fantasy Premier League Managers

Fantasy Football Scout is offering FPL managers an opportunity to get personalized advice through a live Rate My Team surgery featuring Tom Freeman, Scout's Deputy Editor. This session provides a direct line to experienced guidance at a critical point in the season.

## Meet Your Expert

Tom Freeman brings substantial credentials to this role. He is a five-time top 1k finisher in Fantasy Premier League, demonstrating consistent success in one of the most competitive fantasy sports platforms in the world. His expertise combines practical manager experience with editorial oversight at Fantasy Football Scout.

## What the Session Covers

During the live Rate My Team surgery, managers can submit questions covering the full range of pre-deadline decisions. The session addresses questions on transfers, team selection, and captaincy, as well as any other dilemmas managers face as they finalize their squads. Whether managers are unsure about a differential pick, need confirmation on their starting XI, or are weighing captain options, the session provides targeted advice.

## Benefits for Premium Members

Premium Members of Fantasy Football Scout receive priority access during the Rate My Team session. Tom Freeman will be on hand to first reply to requests from Premium Members in the Hot Topic section below the line of this piece. This ensures that paying members get timely, detailed responses to their specific squad concerns before the deadline.

## Additional Tools Available

Beyond the live session, Fantasy Football Scout offers a Rate My Team algorithmic tool available in the Premium Members Area. This tool provides another layer of squad analysis, allowing members to evaluate their teams systematically alongside the personalized advice from the live surgery.

## Staying Informed

Managers can also follow Friday's live team news, which helps inform final decisions before deadline. Combining real-time team news with expert advice and algorithmic analysis creates a comprehensive preparation toolkit for FPL managers.
