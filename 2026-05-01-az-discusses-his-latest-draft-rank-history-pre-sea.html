A Look Back at FPL History

Az's FPL career spans multiple seasons, with notable achievements mixed alongside more challenging periods. His best ever FPL season saw him finish 800th, demonstrating his ability to compete at a high level. Earlier in his career, he achieved a top 5k finish in the 2010/11 season, specifically finishing 3,800th, which represented a strong start to his FPL journey. Another significant milestone came in 2014 when he finished 2k, adding to his list of accomplishments within the FPL community.

Involvement with the FPL Community

Az has become a recognized figure within the broader FPL content creation space. He works for the FPL Show and Scout, where the Premier League markets him as an 'expert'. However, this visibility has not been without challenges. There is quite a lot of negativity around content creators, and Az has personally faced this on social media regarding his FPL content creator role.

He describes himself as an FPL enthusiast rather than an expert, noting that "enthusiast" is probably more accurate to what he is. This self-assessment provides context for his approach heading into the new season as he looks to improve his results while continuing to share his perspective with the FPL community.
