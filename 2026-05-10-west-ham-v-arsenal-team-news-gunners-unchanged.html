West Ham v Arsenal Team News: Gunners Unchanged

Arsenal Starting XI

Arsenal heads into this matchup with an unchanged side from the one that secured victory against Atletico Madrid in midweek. The Gunners have retained the same eleven players that started that Champions League fixture, signaling Mikel Arteta's continued faith in his preferred lineup.

Arsenal Tactical Shape

In defense, Riccardo Calafiori continues his spell at left-back. The Italian defender, priced at £5.6m in Fantasy Football Scout leagues, has held onto his starting position in the backline.

Moving into midfield, Myles Lewis-Skelly receives the nod over Martin Zubimendi. The young English midfielder, valued at £5.0m, has been preferred in the central areas to anchor the Arsenal midfield.

In attack, Bukayo Saka, Eberechi Eze, and Leandro Trossard are deployed in supporting roles behind striker Viktor Gyokeres. This attacking quartet looks to provide the Gunners' firepower for the match.

West Ham United Changes

West Ham United makes two alterations to their side. Jean-Clair Todibo and Aaron Wan-Bissaka come into the starting lineup, replacing Kyle Walker-Peters and Pablo Felipe respectively.

West Ham Formation

The Hammers are set to line up with a back three formation. This tactical setup suggests a defensive approach from the home side looking to frustrate Arsenal's attacking players.

Match Details

Kick-off is scheduled for 16:30 BST.
