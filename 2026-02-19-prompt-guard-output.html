Prompt Guard: Output Protection - The Real Opportunity

While everyone focuses on protecting AI from malicious inputs, the bigger opportunity is protecting customers from what AI says. Here's why output protection matters more.

The Problem

Your AI customer support agent might say:

  • "I can give you 30% off" (unauthorised commitment)
  • "Our system uses GPT-4" (AI identity leak)
  • "The price is actually £X" (pricing leak)
  • "I recommend investing in..." (illegal advice)
  • "john@example.com" (PII exposure)

Why Output Protection is the Opportunity

Input Protection

Competitors: Aegis, llm-guard, rebuff, superagent

Output Protection

Few competitors. Clearer ROI. Compliance teams have budget.

Business Value

Output protection directly impacts:

  • Compliance - GDPR, consumer protection laws
  • Brand reputation - No inappropriate AI responses
  • Legal exposure - AI making commitments
  • Customer trust - Safe interactions

These teams have budget. They need solutions. And existing tools don't focus here.

The Take

Input protection is a commodity. Output protection is the opportunity. Fewer competitors, clearer ROI, and compliance teams are desperate for solutions.
