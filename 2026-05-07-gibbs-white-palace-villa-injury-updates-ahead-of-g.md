# Gameweek 36 Injury Updates: Gibbs-White, Palace and Villa Fitness Check

With Fantasy Premier League managers weighing up their squads ahead of Gameweek 36, several key players across Nottingham Forest, Crystal Palace, and Aston Villa face uncertain or confirmed fitness situations heading into Thursday's matches.

## Nottingham Forest

**Morgan Gibbs-White** (£7.7m) is a notable fitness doubt. The Forest midfielder sustained a facial injury during Monday's win over Chelsea, and manager Matheus Pereira fielded several questions about his availability at the pre-match press conference. The assessment amounts to a significant question mark over his participation.

**Zach Abbott** (£3.8m) suffered a head injury in Gameweek 35, though Pereira expressed confidence in his condition. The defender is expected to be available for Thursday's fixture.

Elsewhere in the Forest squad, **Callum Hudson-Odoi** (muscle), **John Victor** (knee), **Willy Boly** (knee), and **Nicola Savona** (knee) remain long-term absentees. **Murillo** (muscle), **Ola Aina** (unspecified), **Dan Ndoye** (unspecified), and **Ibrahim Sangaré** (unspecified) all missed Gameweek 35, and their fitness for Thursday remains uncertain.

## Crystal Palace

**Evann Guessand** (£6.2m) has returned to training following a knee injury but will not feature in the Conference League semi-final second leg against Shakhtar Donetsk. It remains unclear whether his participation in the Premier League fixture is being considered separately.

**Borna Sosa** (£4.8m) has not recovered from a minor issue and was unavailable for the match against Bournemouth. He remains unavailable.

**Will Hughes** (£4.7m) has recovered from illness and is available for selection.

**Eddie Nketiah** (hamstring) and **Cheick Doucoure** (knee/muscle/match fitness) continue to be unavailable.

## Aston Villa

**Amadou Onana** (£4.8m) will miss out again and is unavailable for Aston Villa's rematch with Nottingham Forest. He was not present in training earlier in the day.

**John McGinn** (£5.3m) has returned to training and is expected to be available for the clash with Nottingham Forest, having trained normally.

Managers considering Forest and Villa assets will want to confirm final line-ups before the Gameweek 36 deadline, with Gibbs-White's status and the cluster of uncertain Forest defenders representing the most pressing decisions.
