The reasoning behind targeting these two goalkeepers extends beyond simple clean sheet percentages. This statistical anomaly actually enhances Newcastle's appeal because it suggests they may keep clean sheets more frequently than the historical record implies, while the fixture still carries the offensive potential that could yield bonus points.
