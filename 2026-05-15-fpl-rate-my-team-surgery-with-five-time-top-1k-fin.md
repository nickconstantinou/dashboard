# FPL Rate My Team Surgery with Tom Freeman

## Meet Your FPL Expert

Tom Freeman brings significant experience to Fantasy Football Scout's Rate My Team surgery. He is a five-time top 1k finisher, demonstrating consistent success in Fantasy Premier League competitions. Freeman also serves as the Deputy Editor of Fantasy Football Scout, where he applies his expertise to help fantasy managers improve their squads.

## How the Rate My Team Session Works

The Rate My Team surgery provides Fantasy Premier League managers with direct access to Freeman's insights. Premium Members receive priority access to this service, with their requests addressed first in the Hot Topic forum. This ensures dedicated members get timely responses to their pre-deadline dilemmas.

Beyond the live surgery, Fantasy Football Scout offers a Rate My Team algorithmic tool in the Premium Members Area. This allows members to evaluate their squad selections at any time, independent of the live session schedule.
