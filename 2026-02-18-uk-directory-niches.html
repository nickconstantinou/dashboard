Why These Work

  • Local focus: UK-specific (unlike US-centric directories)
  • High intent: People searching when they NEED a service
  • Trust matters: Ratings and reviews = massive value in UK
  • GDPR opportunity: Verified businesses, not user-generated

Monetisation for UK

  • Lead gen: ¬£5-50 per lead depending on industry
  • Premium listings: ¬£50-200/month for featured businesses
  • Call tracking: Track leads and charge per conversion
  • Affiliate: Insurance, finance referrals
