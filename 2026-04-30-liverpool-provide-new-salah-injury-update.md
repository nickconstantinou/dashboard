# Liverpool Provide New Mohamed Salah Injury Update

## Injury Diagnosis

Liverpool have confirmed that Mohamed Salah (£14.0m) has been diagnosed with a muscle injury. The issue forced the forward off during Gameweek 34 action, raising concerns among Fantasy Premier League managers who have invested heavily in the Egyptian international.

## Nature of the Issue

The club has described the injury as relatively minor in nature. This assessment will come as reassuring news to Fantasy managers, given Salah's status as one of the most expensive assets in the game at £14.0m.

## Return Timeline

Liverpool expects Salah to be available to play again before the end of this season. This update provides clarity for managers weighing up whether to maintain their investment in the forward for the remaining fixtures.

## Season Context

The injury news arrives as Salah is expected to leave Liverpool in the summer. Fantasy managers should factor this into their long-term planning, as his departure will have significant implications for the FPL market heading into next season.

## Manager's Press Conference

Arne Slot will face the media at 9am BST on Friday, where further details about Salah's recovery and potential return date may be addressed.
