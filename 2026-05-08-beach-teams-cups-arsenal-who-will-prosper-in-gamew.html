The Run-In Context

Arsenal has reached the Champions League final, adding a major European competition to their remaining fixtures. Within this context, Bukayo Saka (£9.9m) is back and immediately looking excellent after his recovery from injury. The England international has four goals and two assists from his latest three games against Sunday's opponents, West Ham United. This return to form makes Saka a particularly attractive asset for managers looking to capitalise on Arsenal's remaining fixtures while his team balances domestic and European commitments.

Aston Villa's European Final

Aston Villa will contest a European final between Gameweeks 37 and 38, creating similar selection considerations to Arsenal. This could significantly impact the reliability of Aston Villa assets in the closing weeks of the season.

The Cup Final Calendar

The final fixtures of the season are complicated by three major cup finals that fall immediately after Gameweek 38. The FA Cup final takes place on the Saturday of Gameweek 37, potentially affecting players from Manchester United and Manchester City. Crystal Palace's Conference League final will be three days after Gameweek 38, which could influence squad selection for Palace in their closing league matches. The Champions League final takes place six days after Gameweek 38, meaning Arsenal players involved may have their preparation affected in the season's closing stages.

Key Gameweek 37 Fixtures

In Gameweek 37, Chelsea host Spurs while Man City travel to Bournemouth. This fixture combination presents interesting opportunities, particularly for City assets travelling to the South Coast. The presence of these high-powered attacks in favourable matchups could make this round particularly productive for managers with exposure to either side.

This historical trend often makes Gameweek 38 attractive for managers looking to maximise returns through attacking players. Combined with potential squad rotation in earlier Gameweeks due to cup commitments, this final day pattern could make differential selections particularly valuable.
