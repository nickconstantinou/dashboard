# The Scout Squad: Our Top Picks for FPL Gameweek 37

## Introduction

Fantasy Premier League managers seeking differential options for Gameweek 37 can find value across multiple price points. The Scout Squad has identified several players who offer strong underlying statistics and potential returns based on their recent form and fixtures.

## Mid-Priced Manchester City Asset

Nico O'Reilly has emerged as a budget option from Manchester City with impressive recent returns. In his last nine outings, O'Reilly has accumulated 71 points, scoring four goals and keeping five clean sheets. Available at £5.3m, he represents one of the cheapest routes into the Manchester City defence while maintaining consistent point returns. His versatility across the backline and attacking contribution make him a viable option for managers seeking value in the final fixtures.

## Everton Midfielders

Everton offers two midfield options worth considering for this gameweek. Kiernan Dewsbury-Hall has generated notable attacking statistics since Gameweek 29, registering eight shots on target during that period. Notably, only two midfielders of any price bracket have bettered this tally, highlighting his underlying creative quality at the £5.2m price point.

The Brighton keeper has recorded five clean sheets across his 10 appearances this season, averaging a shutout every two matches. His consistent run in the side has provided reliable returns for managers who selected him as an affordable backend option.

Keane Lewis-Potter returned to action in Keith Andrews' Brentford lineup during Gameweek 30. Managers with premium funds available may consider Gyokeres for his fixture-proof goal threat.

## Conclusion

Gameweek 37 presents opportunities across various price ranges. Budget managers can target O'Reilly or Lewis-Potter for differential returns, while those seeking midfield value should note Dewsbury-Hall's strong chance creation metrics.
