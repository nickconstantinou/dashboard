Modern AI systems require massive 3D memory chips built vertically like skyscrapers to store all that training data, and these chips need to be carved using beams of light through a process called etching.

Here's where it gets interesting: when you're beaming photons down a vertical stack at temperatures around 50 degrees, the materials used become incredibly important.
