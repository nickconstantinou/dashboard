AI News Roundup: DeepMind's Moral AI

Here's what's happening in the world of AI:

Meta Plans $65 Million on Elections to Sway AI Legislation

Meta is planning to spend $65 million on elections to influence AI legislation. The funding will go toward pro-AI super PACs, including two new ones: Republican-focused "Forge the Future Project" and Democrat-focused "Making Our Tomorrow."

OpenAI Hires Instagram's VP of Global Partnerships

OpenAI has hired Charles Porch, who helped land Instagram's biggest partnerships, as its first VP of global creative partnerships.

Epic Games Acquires Meshcapade for AI Animation Tech

Epic is acquiring Meshcapade, a company that makes AI tech for animating digital humans. The team will join Epic Games' AI Research team.

Lyria 3: AI-Generated Music from Google

Google's Lyria 3 AI model can generate 30-second tracks based on images, videos, and text descriptions.

AI Search Spam Concerns

Chatbots could bring back search spam, as journalists found confident but incorrect AI-generated attributions.

Sources: The Verge, TechCrunch
