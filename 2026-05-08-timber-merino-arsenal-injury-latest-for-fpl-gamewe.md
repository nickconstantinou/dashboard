# Arsenal Injury Latest: Timber and Merino Ruled Out for GW36

## Overview

Arsenal manager Mikel Arteta confirmed there are no fresh injury concerns following the club's midweek Champions League victory over Atletico Madrid.

## Jurrien Timber Status

Jurrien Timber continues to be unavailable due to an ankle injury. The defender has been ruled out for the upcoming Gameweek 36 fixture, with Arteta stating there is "no chance for the weekend." Timber's recovery timeline remains uncertain beyond this setback.

## Mikel Merino Status

Mikel Merino will also miss Gameweek 36 due to a foot injury. Like Timber, Arteta has indicated the midfielder has "no chance for the weekend" and will not be available for selection this Gameweek.

## Season Outlook

Both players face uncertain prospects for the remainder of the campaign. When asked whether either Timber or Merino would feature again this season, Arteta responded: "I don't know. There's still a fair bit to do, so everything has to be so smooth and quick if they want to have a chance of playing minutes."

## Fantasy Premier League Implications

For FPL managers considering Arsenal assets, neither Timber nor Merino represent viable options for Gameweek 36. The uncertainty around their season-long availability may influence squad planning for the remaining fixtures.
