Team News Round-Up: Welbeck Returns as Gordon and Hall Remain Substitutes

Brighton: Welbeck Back in Starting XI

Danny Welbeck returns to Brighton's starting lineup after missing the midweek Double Gameweek 33 fixtures. His recall comes at the expense of Georginio Rutter, who drops out of the starting XI. Rutter had been priced at £5.6m for Fantasy Football purposes.

Newcastle United: Gordon Returns, Hall Remains on Bench

Anthony Gordon has recovered from a hip injury and returns to the Newcastle squad, though he begins this match on the bench. His price point of £7.4m makes him a notable Fantasy asset if he can regain full fitness. Meanwhile, Lewis Hall must settle for a substitute role for the second consecutive match. Hall's price sits at £5.3m, and his continued absence from the starting XI represents a consideration for Fantasy managers.

Also for Newcastle, Joelinton comes back into the starting lineup after serving a two-match suspension. He replaces Jacob Ramsey in the XI.

Wolves: Multiple Changes Under Edwards

Wolves manager Rob Edwards has made several changes to his starting lineup for this match. Dan Bentley, Yerson Mosquera, and Tolu Arokodare have all been promoted to the starting XI. In contrast, Matt Doherty and Rodrigo Gomes drop to the bench.

Goalkeeper Jose Sa misses the match through injury, creating an opportunity for Bentley's inclusion in the lineup.

Sunderland: Le Bris Rings the Changes

Sunderland manager Regis Le Bris has responded to his team's heavy defeat against Nottingham Forest by making substitutions in the starting XI. Habib Diarra and Chris Rigg have been replaced by Reinildo and Chemsdine Talbi.

Brentford vs West Ham: Unchanged Lineups

Both Brentford and West Ham United name unchanged XIs for this fixture, providing consistency for Fantasy managers tracking these squads.
