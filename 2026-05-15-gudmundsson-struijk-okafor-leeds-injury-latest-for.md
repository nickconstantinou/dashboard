# Leeds United Injury Latest for FPL Gameweek 37

Leeds United are facing a mounting injury crisis ahead of a crucial Premier League fixture, with manager Daniel Farke describing the situation as the most difficult the club has faced during the entire season.

## Multiple Players Ruled Out

Four Leeds players remain unavailable through injury heading into Gameweek 37. Gabriel Gudmundsson (£3.8m) continues his recovery from a hamstring problem, joining Ilia Gruev (£4.7m) on the sidelines. Gruev, who has been sidelined with a hamstring injury, is also confirmed to be out for the remainder of the season. Further absences include Jayden Bogle (£4.4m), who is also dealing with a hamstring issue, and Noah Okafor (£5.5m), who has been nursing a calf problem.

The cluster of injuries leaves Farke with limited options in several areas of his squad.

## Doubts Over Defensive Options

Beyond those confirmed absences, Pascal Struijk (£4.2m) is a major doubt for the fixture. The defender has not trained this week due to a hip complaint, making his involvement uncertain. Similarly, Ethan Ampadu (£4.9m) is now a major doubt after being struck down by illness, further depleting Farke's defensive resources.

## Loanee Unavailable

Facundo Buonanotte (£4.6m) will not be available for selection as he is ineligible to face his parent club, a situation that removes another attacking option from Leeds' plans.

## Manager's Assessment

Farke has acknowledged the severity of the situation, stating that the current injury landscape represents perhaps the most difficult circumstances the club has encountered this season.

## FPL Implications

For Fantasy Premier League managers considering Leeds assets, the injury list significantly narrows viable options. With multiple defenders ruled out and key midfielders uncertain, those with Leeds coverage may need to explore alternatives or wait for confirmation before the deadline.
