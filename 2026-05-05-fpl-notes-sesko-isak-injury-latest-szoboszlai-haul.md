# FPL Notes: Sesko and Isak Injury Updates, Szoboszlai Haul

Fantasy Premier League managers face key decisions around Liverpool and Manchester United assets following the latest injury updates and standout performances from the weekend action.

## Benjamin Sesko Shin Injury Concern

Benjamin Sesko was withdrawn at half-time during Manchester United's match, having sustained a shin problem. The forward had found the net before being replaced, but the concern centers on the nature of the injury.

According to reports, Sesko had been carrying a shin issue before picking up another knock on the same spot. This pre-existing condition means United have been managing him carefully, and the fresh blow to the same area compounds the problem. Sesko is priced at £7.3m and represented a budget-friendly route into the United attack for FPL managers.

## Alexander Isak Misses Out with Groin Issue

Alexander Isak was absent from Liverpool's squad at Old Trafford, missing the match due to a minor groin problem. The Swedish striker, priced at £10.3m, has been a regular starter this season but could not feature in this fixture.

The situation is being monitored closely, with the club hoping Isak will be ready for the following week. Managers will need to assess his availability before the season concludes. The same timeframe applies to Alisson Becker, who has not yet trained with the team, making his immediate availability unlikely.

Alisson's lack of training involvement suggests a straightforward decision for Liverpool's coaching staff—he will not feature until he completes at least partial training with the squad. The goalkeeper is priced at £5.4m.

## Dominik Szoboszlai Delivers Haul

Dominik Szoboszlai provided significant value for Liverpool assets with a standout performance, scoring a superb individual goal before unselfishly assisting Cody Gakpo. The midfielder finished the match with 13 points, a tally that will have rewarded managers who selected him.

Szoboszlai is priced at £7.0m and offers an attacking route into Liverpool's midfield. His haul came at a crucial stage of the season as Liverpool continue their push.

## Champions League Qualification Context

Liverpool probably still need a few more points to secure Champions League qualification for next season. The club's remaining fixtures are Chelsea, Aston Villa, and Brentford to finish the campaign. These matches will determine whether Liverpool secures its position in Europe's premier competition.

Manchester United, meanwhile, will return to the UEFA Champions League next season, with qualification confirmed for the Red Devils.
