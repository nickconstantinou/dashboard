# Arsenal v Newcastle United: Team News and Starting Lineups

## Arsenal Team News

Bukayo Saka returns to the Arsenal matchday squad for the first time since March. The England international is among the substitutes this evening. Saka, priced at £9.8m in Fantasy Premier League, provides a significant boost to Arsenal's attacking options as they face Newcastle United.

Manager Mikel Arteta had indicated that Riccardo Calafiori would likely be available, but the Italian defender is not in the squad for this match. Calafiori, priced at £5.6m, will miss out despite recent positive assessments of his fitness.

## Arsenal Starting XI

Arsenal's confirmed lineup features David Raya in goal. Ben White replaces Cristhian Mosquera at right-back in what appears to be a tactical adjustment to the backline. The full starting XI is: Raya, White, Saliba, Gabriel, Hincapie, Rice, Zubimendi, Odegaard, Eze, Madueke, Havertz.

## Newcastle United Team News

Nick Pope replaces Aaron Ramsdale in goal for Newcastle, priced at £5.0m. Pope returns between the posts as Newcastle look to secure a result at the Emirates Stadium.

Dan Burn, Joe Willock, and Jacob Murphy also earn recalls to the starting lineup. Tino Livramento misses out because of a thigh injury. The Newcastle defender, priced at £4.9m, will be unavailable for this fixture.

Bruno Guimaraes starts for the first time since February. The Brazilian midfielder marked his comeback from injury with a Gameweek 33 cameo and now returns to the starting eleven, providing creativity and defensive solidity in the center of the park.

## Newcastle Starting XI

Newcastle's confirmed lineup is: Pope, Miley, Thiaw, Botman, Burn, Guimaraes, Tonali, Ramsey, J Murphy, Osula, Willock.
