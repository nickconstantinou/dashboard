Vibe Coding Is the New Product Management

When anyone can describe what they want in natural language, the skill shifts from writing syntax to articulating vision. Product management becomes less about translating requirements into technical specs and more about clear thinking about outcomes.

Training Models Is the New Coding

The abstraction layer has moved. Instead of writing code that runs once, you're creating systems that learn and improve. The programmer of the future trains; they don't just write.

The English Programming Language

Natural language is becoming the interface to computation. This democratizes access but raises the bar for precise thinking‚Äîambiguity in thought now leads directly to ambiguity in output.

AI Adapts to Us Faster Than We Adapt to It

The rate of AI improvement is staggering. Each generation better understands context, intent, and nuance. The humans who thrive will be those who learn to collaborate with AI rather than compete against it.

The Goal Is Not to Have a Job

Naval's provocative take: worrying about AI taking your job misses the point. The goal was never employment‚Äîit was agency and creation. AI can be the tool that finally makes that accessible to everyone.

No Entrepreneur Is Worried About AI Taking Their Job

The Solution to AI Anxiety Is Action

Stop theorizing. Start using. The best way to understand AI's limits and strengths is to work with it directly.
