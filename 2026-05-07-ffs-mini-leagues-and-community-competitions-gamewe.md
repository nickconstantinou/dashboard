# FFS Mini-Leagues and Community Competitions – Gameweek 35

## Hall of Fame Highlights

The FFS Live Hall of Fame now ranks 55,802 FPL managers based on results through Gameweek 35. Ben Crellin has secured 7th position in the live Hall of Fame with an overall rank of 73,819, achieved through the use of a Wildcard chip. The Hall of Fame continues to track managers participating in both the Fantasy Football Scout and Fantasy Football Scout Members mini-leagues.

## Chip Strategy Performance

Stoyan Kolarov emerged as the highest scorer in Gameweek 35, accumulating 78 points from a Bench Boost activation. This represents one of the standout individual performances among community managers for the round.

## Captaincy and Player Performance

Noah Okafor (£5.6m) was selected as captain by several managers and delivered double-digit returns, helping those managers rise to 4,983rd overall. Marcos Senesi (£5.2m) also achieved double-digit points in Gameweek 35, joining Okafor among the managers who reached that 4,983rd position.

## FFS Members Cup

Gameweek 35 marked round five of the 20th FFS Members Cup. DaveZubie, one of the best-ranked managers in the competition, was defeated by Team Dependant with a 56-58 score. The quarter-final stage featured J Aravind as the highest-ranked remaining player at 67,802nd overall, who has previously achieved three top 20k finishes.

## Community Mini-League Standings

In League 1, Alex Merchant has become the new leader with 69 points and an overall rank of 1,755th. Andreas Veggeland continues to lead the Fantasy Football Scout mini-league (code ql9e6k) for the second consecutive week, marking his third time at the top this season. Veggeland has reached 40th position worldwide, demonstrating strong consistency in the community competition.
