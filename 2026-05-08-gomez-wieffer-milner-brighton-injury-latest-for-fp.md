# Brighton Injury Latest for FPL Gameweek 36

## Captain's Return on the Horizon

Lewis Dunk, Brighton's captain and priced at £4.5m, has had limited minutes recently due to knee niggles and a suspension. However, there is positive news ahead of Gameweek 36. The defender could return this weekend after having an extra week of training, which manager Fabian Hurzeler described as a positive step in his recovery.

## Wieffer Assessment Ahead

Mats Wieffer, valued at £4.9m, has a small ankle issue and will be assessed before the match. Speaking at Friday's press conference, Hurzeler indicated it would be tight for this game, noting the issue is "nothing serious" but requiring further evaluation.

## Returning Players

James Milner and Solly March are set to return to contention in Gameweek 36. Milner's absence was unspecified, while March had been dealing with a muscle issue.

## Long-Term Absentees

Stefanos Tzimas and Adam Webster remain longer-term absentees, both recovering from knee injuries.

## Diego Gomez Update

Diego Gomez continues his recovery from a knee injury. Manager Hurzeler expressed cautious optimism, stating: "I'm hoping [Gomez will be fit] but I think it's important that we understand that we can't risk his health." The Wolverhampton Wanderers match may come too soon for the midfielder.
