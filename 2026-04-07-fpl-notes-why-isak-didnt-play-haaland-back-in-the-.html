FPL Notes: Haaland Hat-Trick Sparks Revival as Man City Dismantles Liverpool

The Full Story

Manchester City delivered a commanding 4-0 victory over Liverpool at the Etihad Stadium on April 6, 2026, with Erling Haaland scoring a hat-trick to secure the club's eighth consecutive FA Cup semi-final appearance. The Norwegian striker converted a penalty while adding two more goals between the 39th and 57th minutes, transforming what could have been a different narrative when Mohamed Salah squandered an early chance after taking an uncharacteristic extra touch. Defender Abdukodir Khusanov (£5.4m) blocked Salah's 15th-minute opportunity, allowing Haaland to seize control of the match.

Antoine Semenyo (£8.2m) and Nico O'Reilly (£5.0m) were the standout performers supporting Haaland's attacking masterclass. Semenyo, who was the most-sold individual heading into Blank Gameweek 31, delivered two assists—crossing for Haaland's glancing header and then dinking over goalkeeper Giorgi Marmardashvili after meeting Rayan Cherki's through ball. O'Reilly, despite being listed as a left-back, operated high up the pitch and earned the penalty that led to Haaland's fourth goal, having also scored twice in the EFL Cup final.
