# Manchester United Injury Latest for FPL Gameweek 37

## Casemiro Available for Final Home Match

Manchester United midfielder Casemiro is available for selection ahead of Gameweek 37. The Brazilian veteran missed Gameweek 36 due to a minor issue but has returned to full availability. Notably, this upcoming fixture will be Casemiro's final home match as a Manchester United player.

## Manuel Ugarte Returns to Training

Manuel Ugarte has also trained this week and is available for Gameweek 37 selection. The midfielder missed both Gameweek 35 and Gameweek 36 but appears ready to return to action. Ugarte's availability provides Manchester United with additional midfield options for their upcoming fixture.

## Benjamin Sesko Remains a Doubt

Benjamin Sesko (£7.3m) is still not fully fit heading into Gameweek 37. The forward has been managing a shin problem and remains short of 100% fitness. FPL managers considering Sesko as a budget forward option should factor in this ongoing injury concern.

## Matthijs de Ligt Confirmed Out for Season

Defender Matthijs de Ligt (£4.9m) will miss the remainder of the season due to a back injury. The Dutch centre-back is unavailable for the final gameweek of the season, eliminating him from FPL consideration for the campaign's conclusion.
