Liverpool Injury Latest for FPL Gameweek 37

This article provides the latest injury updates from Liverpool ahead of Fantasy Premier League Gameweek 37, drawing from the most recent reports.

Mohamed Salah

Mohamed Salah (£14.0m) will return to the Liverpool squad for Gameweek 37 following his hamstring injury, though he is expected to feature only as a substitute. The Egyptian international is available for only a few minutes, with reports indicating: "Mo will be available tomorrow for only a few minutes but hopefully, he can come in."

Florian Wirtz

Florian Wirtz (£8.3m) is not yet fully recovered from a stomach bug and remains on antibiotics. He will be a late call for Gameweek 37. Reports state: "Florian had a stomach infection. Let's see how fit he is for tomorrow. He is on antibiotics, so let's see if he's ready to play tomorrow."

Ibrahima Konate

Ibrahima Konate is available after cramp forced him off during Gameweek 36. The defender has been confirmed as okay, with reports indicating: "Ibou is okay, he trained with us."

Goalkeeper Update

Alisson (muscle) has returned to training but it remains uncertain whether he will feature in Gameweek 37. Reports state: "Alisson trains with us again, let's see where he is tomorrow, if he is ready or has to wait for one more week."

Players Remaining Out

Several Liverpool players continue their recovery and will not be available for Gameweek 37. The confirmed absentees are: Wataru Endo (foot), Hugo Ekitike (Achilles), Conor Bradley (knee), Stefan Bajcetic (hamstring), Jayden Danns (hamstring), and Giovanni Leoni (knee).
