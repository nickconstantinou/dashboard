# Gueye, Branthwaite, Grealish: Everton Injury Latest for FPL Gameweek 36

Idrissa Gana Gueye remains a doubt for Gameweek 36, according to the latest update from Fantasy Football Scout. The Senegalese midfielder sat out the six-goal thriller with Manchester City with a slight injury and hasn't trained since. Everton manager David Moyes has said the injury is not serious, and the club is hopeful Gueye will be back soon.

Gueye carries a price tag of £5.4m and could be a consideration for FPL managers assessing their midfielder options ahead of the Gameweek 36 deadline.

Jarrad Branthwaite remains sidelined with a hamstring injury. The defender, priced at £5.3m, continues to be unavailable for the Toffees.

Jack Grealish also remains sidelined with a foot injury. The midfielder, valued at £6.3m, is not expected to return for Gameweek 36.
