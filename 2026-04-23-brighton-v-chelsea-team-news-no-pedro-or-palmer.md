# Brighton v Chelsea Team News: No Pedro or Palmer

## Key Absences Confirmed

Chelsea will be without both Cole Palmer and Joao Pedro for tonight's Premier League fixture against Brighton. Cole Palmer is out due to a hamstring injury, having suffered tightness that has kept him from participating in this game.

Joao Pedro is also unavailable for Chelsea, though the situation differs slightly. While he has a hamstring issue, reports suggest he is expected to be fit for Sunday but this game has come too close for him to be involved.

Both absences are enforced changes for Chelsea, as confirmed by the manager's lineup announcement.

## Chelsea Starting XI Changes

Chelsea make two changes to their side for this match. Romeo Lavia and Trevoh Chalobah come into the starting eleven. These alterations are enforced due to the injuries to Palmer and Estevao.

The confirmed line-up means Chelsea must reshuffle their approach in attack and midfield for this fixture.

## Brighton Team Selection

Brighton make three changes to their starting eleven for tonight's game. Georgino Rutter starts and replaces Danny Welbeck, who is benched and not starting. Kaoru Mitoma returns to the starting XI at the expense of the sidelined Diego Gomez. Carlos Baleba replaces Yasin Ayari as the other alteration.

Both clubs are playing their second fixture of Double Gameweek 33.

## Fixture Details

Kick-off is scheduled for 20:00 BST. The match is part of the Premier League schedule, with both teams managing their squads across this busy period following their previous DGW33 fixture.
