## Free Hit Strategy

In this article, Zophar reveals his approach to navigating Gameweek 34. Most FPL managers will be activating their Free Hit chip this week, including Zophar himself. The Free Hit chip allows managers to field a completely new team for a single gameweek while retaining their original squad for future gameweeks, making it an ideal tool for blank gameweeks where fewer players are available.
