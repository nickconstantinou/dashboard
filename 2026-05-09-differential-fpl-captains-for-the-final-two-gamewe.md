# Differential FPL Captains for the Final Two Gameweeks

## Jarrod Bowen – West Ham (£7.7m)

Jarrod Bowen has been a consistent point-scoring asset for Fantasy managers throughout the season. The West Ham talisman has delivered an attacking return or defensive contribution points in 10 of his last 14 matches, demonstrating his reliability as a 90-minute performer.

In that 14-match span, Bowen has amassed 81 points—the fourth-most of any player in the game. This places him among the most productive assets available to Fantasy managers.

Looking ahead, managers should note that Bowen racked up 13 points in Gameweek 38 last season, suggesting he can deliver when stakes are high in the final matches.

## Mohamed Salah – Liverpool (£14.0m)

Concerns about Mohamed Salah's fitness may be overstated. His muscle issue is relatively minor in nature, and the club are confident he'll feature again this season. Managers holding Salah need not panic, though the timeline for his return remains unclear.

## Dango Ouattara – Brentford (£5.7m)

Among all Fantasy midfielders who have made more than five starts, Dango Ouattara ranks fifth for most big chances per 90 minutes (0.68). This underlying metric suggests the Brentford midfielder creates quality opportunities and could be a differential option for managers looking away from popular picks.

## Viktor Gyokeres

Despite much criticism, Gyokeres actually has the fifth-best minutes per goal ratio this season among players with a minimum of 500 minutes. Additionally, 11 of his 14 Premier League goals have come against teams currently in the bottom half, indicating he performs best against weaker opposition.

## Arsenal

The Gunners will have enjoyed their first free midweek in a while before the relegated Clarets visit, suggesting a potential fixture advantage heading into their upcoming match.

## Leeds United

Leeds United have kept just two away clean sheets all season, highlighting their vulnerability on the road.

## Bruno Fernandes

Bruno Fernandes (£10.4m) will probably be the stand-out captaincy option in Gameweek 37, especially if Nottingham Forest are safe from relegation.
