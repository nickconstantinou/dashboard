# FPL Blank Gameweek 34: Best Players to Buy Without Chips

## Arsenal's Favorable Fixture Run

Arsenal have three home matches in the next four Gameweeks, hosting Newcastle United, Fulham and Burnley. This fixture run makes Gunners assets particularly attractive for managers without chips to deploy. The North London side's upcoming schedule provides a window of opportunity to target players from their squad strategically.

## Declan Rice: Midfield Engine

Declan Rice (£7.2m) presents a compelling option for managers navigating a blank Gameweek 34. He has met the defensive contribution (DefCon) threshold in five matches since the turn of the year, demonstrating consistent underlying value beyond just goal involvement. Additionally, Rice ranks joint-second among all players for chances created with 61, highlighting his dual threat in both defensive and attacking phases. His ability to accumulate FPL points through multiple channels makes him a reliable option during this period.

## Everton: Defensive Strength Away From Home

Everton have suffered only two losses since November. The Toffees are unbeaten in seven of their last eight matches away from home. Furthermore, only Arsenal and Man City have conceded fewer goals per 90 (p90) in away matches this season than Everton. This defensive solidity suggests their players could be undervalued options for Blank Gameweek 34.

## James Garner: Defensive Midfield Asset

James Garner (£5.3m) offers exceptional value for managers seeking budget options. He is second among midfielders for DefCon points this season, making him an excellent source of defensive returns at a bargain price. His consistent ability to meet DefCon thresholds provides a reliable floor of points even in difficult matchups.

## Kiernan Dewsbury-Hall: Budget Attacking Option

Kiernan Dewsbury-Hall (£5.1m) represents an affordable route into Everton's midfield. He has scored three goals and supplied three assists in his last nine appearances, indicating recent attacking output that belies his budget price tag. For managers unable to deploy chips, his combination of low cost and recent form offers flexibility in constructing a squad around higher-value assets.

## Important Consideration

Jarrad Branthwaite (£5.3m) will miss the remainder of the season due to injury. While this removes a budget defensive option from consideration, Everton's overall defensive record remains strong for other assets.
