Spurs v Leeds team news: Bogle out, hosts unchanged

📖 Read original on Fantasy Football Scout →

Leeds United Injury Updates

Leeds United head to Tottenham Hotspur Stadium with several absentees. Jayden Bogle is absent with a hamstring issue. The defender, valued at £4.4m in Fantasy terms, will miss the encounter. Noah Okafor is also missing for Leeds United. The forward, priced at £5.6m, was known about in advance of the deadline. Additionally, scarcely used loanee Facundo Buonanotte is absent with a muscle problem. The midfielder carries a £4.6m price tag.

Leeds United Team Selection

Leeds United manager Daniel Farke has resisted rotation despite the visitors having nothing left to play for. His two changes are both enforced thanks to injuries. Dan James and Brenden Aaronson start in their places. Aaronson, valued at £5.4m, will look to provide attacking threat from the outset. The visitors are in good form but they are now mathematically safe, thanks to West Ham United's defeat on Sunday.

Tottenham Hotspur Team News

Spurs boss Roberto de Zerbi has, predictably, kept the same side that beat Aston Villa last time out. The Lilywhites remain unchanged for the fixture. James Maddison is on the bench again for the Lilywhites. The midfielder, priced at £6.8m, continues his recovery and remains unavailable for a starting role.
