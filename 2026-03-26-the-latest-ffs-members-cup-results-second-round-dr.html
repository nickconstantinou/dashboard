The latest FFS Members Cup results + second-round draw

The Fantasy Football Scout Members Cup has completed its first round, with 128 Fantasy Premier League managers advancing to Round 2, set to take place in Gameweek 32. Of the six former champions who entered the competition, only two survived the opening stage. Mayanyi and Drizzle emerged victorious from their respective ties, while Mohd Rodzi, Rossaldinho, rrcmc, and Chabs were all eliminated in round one, losing to BilboBaggins, RamaJama, Joletouriste, and IvanGeorgiev respectively.

The opening round produced a range of scores that highlighted the unpredictable nature of FPL cup competitions. FplSos and Ryan with Podnah led the way with impressive 76-point hauls in Gameweek 31. Wyrd bið ful aræd progressed with 75 points, while Surgical Attack and DrMammon advanced with 74. Notably, luck played a significant role in determining several outcomes. Tieron Nobody was eliminated despite scoring 71 points, falling to bluemurder's 73, while Flying Nugget and norab were knocked out on 69 points. Remarkably, a tally of just 50 points proved sufficient for pk all stars and 100K Short to advance. Five managers progressed with 52 points: ruddjack, FPL Virgin, Milvus, Ckeulen, and Johnny 8.

The competition also demonstrated how overall FPL rank doesn't guarantee cup success. Matthias99th, ranked 1,482nd in the main game, progressed successfully, but the next four highest-ranked managers all fell short. Pulisic 4 President (1,492nd), YNWA91 (1,511th), FPL Niall (3,251st), and Toalie (3,477th) were all eliminated. Meanwhile, ProfessorM, sitting at 4.39 million overall, shocked many by reaching round two and will face The Great statuto (ranked 1.99 million). The standout second-round fixture sees Matthias99th against oscardude1 (16,140th), representing the lowest combined ranks of any pairing.

Why This Matters

The FFS Members Cup represents one of the most significant community-driven competitions in the Fantasy Premier League ecosystem, offering £300 in total prizes split between £150 for first place, £100 for second, and £50 for third. The competition mirrors the official FPL cup format, where managers are randomly drawn against each other and the winner is determined by their gameweek score minus transfer costs. The tournament runs through Gameweek 38, with subsequent rounds scheduled for Gameweeks 33 through 38, culminating in the final and third-place play-off.

    • Context & Background

    Fantasy Football Scout has operated the Members Cup as a supplementary competition to the official FPL game since its inception, serving as both a community engagement tool and a proving ground for managers. The competition follows the same scoring and transfer cost deduction rules as the official FPL cup, making it familiar territory for experienced players. The timing of the competition—running from Gameweek 30 through Gameweek 38—coincides with the business end of the Premier League season, when fixture congestion, player rotation, and blank gameweeks create maximum volatility and unpredictability.
