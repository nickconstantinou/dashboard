Leeds v Burnley: Team News and Lineup Changes

Fixture Details

Leeds United v Burnley gets Gameweek 35 of the Fantasy Premier League underway this Friday evening at Elland Road, with kick-off scheduled for 20:00 BST.

Leeds United Team News

Daniel Farke makes three changes to the side that were beaten by Chelsea in last week's FA Cup semi-final.

Goalkeeper: Karl Darlow replaces Lucas Perri in goal for Leeds United. Darlow is priced at £4.0m in Fantasy Premier League.

Defensive Change: Gabriel Gudmundsson is out with the hamstring injury he suffered at Wembley. Joe Rodon comes into the team in place of Gudmundsson at £3.9m, with James Justin presumably switching over to left wing-back.

Midfield Changes: Anton Stach returns to the starting lineup in place of Brenden Aaronson, who drops to the bench. Stach is available at £4.8m, while Aaronson remains priced at £5.4m.

Fitness Boost: Jaka Bijol and Noah Okafor, who were both injury doubts, are fit to start this evening's match.

Burnley Team News

Burnley's interim coach Mike Jackson names an unchanged XI from Gameweek 33.
