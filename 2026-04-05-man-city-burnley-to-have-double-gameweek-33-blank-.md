# The Full Story

Fantasy Premier League managers have received their first confirmed double gameweek of the season, with Manchester City and Burnley confirmed for Double Gameweek 33 following City's progression to the FA Cup semi-finals. The announcement, made on April 4, 2026 by Fantasy Football Scout, confirms that both sides will play twice during Gameweek 33 before blanking out entirely in Gameweek 34.

The double presents a mixed fixture bag for City, who will face both the league leaders and 19th-placed opposition. Crucially for FPL managers, the Burnley fixture arrives just days before Pep Guardiola's side contest their FA Cup semi-final, potentially limiting minutes for key City players in that matchup. The article notes that City's title rivals Arsenal will need to prevail in their Double Gameweek 33 fixture against City for the title race to remain competitive—if Arsenal lose, the championship would effectively be settled.

Beyond this double, the article confirms City are expected to receive another double gameweek later in the season, likely in Gameweek 36, giving managers a strategic window for investment decisions.
