# The Weekender ep. 32 | Planet FPL 2025/26

## Manchester United's Champions League Position

Manchester United have secured Champions League qualification for next season. The team sits on 61 points with an 11-point lead over Brighton with four games remaining. According to the episode, there is no chance they fail to qualify from that position.

## Casemiro's Standout FPL Season

Casemiro has emerged as the sixth highest scoring midfielder in FPL this season. His impressive tally comes despite underlying metrics that suggest he may have overperformed his expected goals (xG). However, there is a method to his success. Harry Maguire praised Casemiro's set piece timing, noting he is the best at the club in terms of heading. The Brazilian has deliberately worked on set pieces during training ground sessions, refining his timing and runs to overperform his underlying numbers.

## Bruno Fernandes Reaches Milestone

Bruno Fernandes has reached 20 FPL assists this season. This milestone further demonstrates his importance to Manchester United's attacking output and makes him a consistently valuable FPL asset throughout the campaign.

## Tactical Adjustment Against Brentford

Carrick made a tactical adjustment at halftime during the match against Brentford. He changed the system and went towards a back five in the second half. This change was necessary because of problems United were having dealing with Brentford's left-hand side, particularly with King Lewis causing issues.

## Brentford's Late Consolation

Matthijs Jensen scored Brentford's late goal against Manchester United. Jensen is 30 years old, giving the hosts a consolation strike in what was ultimately a victory for United.

## Igor Tiago's Unique Blank

Igor Tiago experienced a remarkable blank in Gameweek 34 against Brentford, recording 0.00 xG. According to the episode, he is the first player ever to watch a game of football and finish with zero xG. This represents an extreme case of a player failing to register any meaningful attacking contribution, making him a cautionary example for FPL managers considering his ownership.
