# Everton Injury Latest for FPL Gameweek 37: Branthwaite, Grealish, Gueye

## Overview

Everton heads into Gameweek 37 with three notable players currently unavailable through injury. Fantasy Premier League managers holding assets from the club will need to assess their options carefully as the season reaches its conclusion.

## Jarrad Branthwaite Remains Sidelined

Defender Jarrad Branthwaite continues to miss action due to a hamstring injury. The centre-back remains unavailable for selection as Everton navigates the final stages of the campaign. Branthwaite is priced at £5.3m in FPL and has therefore not been an option for managers in recent fixtures due to his ongoing fitness issue.

## Jack Grealish Still Out

Jack Grealish is also absent from Everton's squad, with the midfielder dealing with a foot injury. Grealish, priced at £6.3m, has not featured for the Toffees and continues his recovery. Both Branthwaite and Grealish remain sidelined for Everton heading into this gameweek.

## Idrissa Gana Gueye Injury Update

Idrissa Gana Gueye sat out Everton's six-goal thriller with Manchester City with what was described as a "slight" injury. The Senegalese midfielder has not featured since that match and is not fit for Everton's current game.

Gueye is back on the grass in training but is not yet available for selection. The midfielder, priced at £5.4m, is making progress in his recovery but has not returned to full fitness.

There remains an outside chance Gueye could make Everton's match against Tottenham, with manager David Moyes indicating the club will monitor his progress in the coming days.

## Seamus Coleman's Final Home Match

Beyond the injury situation, Seamus Coleman is set to feature in what will be his final home match for Everton. The veteran defender announced earlier this week that he is calling time on his playing career with the club, bringing an end to a distinguished spell at Goodison Park.

Manager David Moyes indicated that Coleman would only receive minutes if it is the right thing to do for the team, not for sentimental reasons. The Irish defender's contribution to the club has been significant, and his final home appearance will carry emotional weight regardless of his involvement on the pitch.

## FPL Considerations

For FPL managers, Everton's injury situation presents limited options heading into Gameweek 37. Neither Branthwaite nor Grealish are viable picks in their current state. Gueye, while showing signs of recovery, is not expected to be available and his potential return against Tottenham falls outside this gameweek.

Those considering Everton assets should monitor the situation ahead of the final fixtures, particularly regarding Gueye's recovery timeline.
