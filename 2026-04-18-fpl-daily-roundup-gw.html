FPL Gameweek 33 Roundup: The Double Gameweek Dilemma

Navigating the Most Complex Fixture Block of the Season

The Fantasy Premier League landscape has reached a critical juncture as managers face one of the most consequential fixture blocks of the 2025-26 season. With Double Gameweek 33 confirmed for six clubs and Blank Gameweek 34 looming with another six teams sitting idle, the decisions made in the coming days could define entire seasons. The question is no longer whether to invest in double gameweek assets but rather how aggressively to pivot toward those with multiple matches and which established stars might be worth benching or even excluding entirely.

The confirmed fixture data reveals a stark division across the league. Gameweek 33 sees Bournemouth, Brighton, Burnley, Chelsea, Leeds, and Manchester City all playing twice, creating an immediate template for managers seeking maximum returns. Conversely, Arsenal, Crystal Palace, Manchester City, and Wolves blank in Gameweek 31, while Bournemouth, Brighton, Burnley, Chelsea, Leeds, and Manchester City will sit idle in Gameweek 34. This oscillation between feast and famine makes chip deployment and transfer timing absolutely critical.
