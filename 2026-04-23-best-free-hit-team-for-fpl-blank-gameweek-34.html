Understanding the Blank Gameweek

Six Premier League teams will not play in Blank Gameweek 34 due to the FA Cup semi-finals. These teams are Manchester City, Chelsea, Bournemouth, Brighton and Hove Albion, Leeds United, and Burnley. Managers using the Free Hit chip must carefully select players from the remaining teams that are scheduled to play.

Goalkeeper Selection

Robin Roefs (£4.8m) represents exceptional value among goalkeepers. He is one of only four goalkeepers to surpass 120 points this season. His team, Sunderland, achieved their sixth home clean sheet of the season in Gameweek 32, which is the joint-third most of any team.

Premium Forward Options

Ollie Watkins (£8.6m) leads the Blank Gameweek 34 transfer activity as the most transferred-in player so far. He has scored three times in his last two trips to Craven Cottage, amassing 21 points from those matches alone.

Morgan Rogers (£7.4m) offers strong value as a mid-priced forward. He has recorded a goal and two assists in his last three matches, demonstrating recent involvement in Aston Villa's attacking play.

Harry Wilson (£6.0m) presents a budget-friendly forward option. He has recorded 10 attacking returns in 15 home appearances at Craven Cottage.

Midfield Strategy

Morgan Gibbs-White (£7.5m) stands out as one of the division's form players. He has racked up 91 points since the turn of the year, which is more than any other asset except Fernandes.

Mohamed Salah (£14.0m) continues his consistent scoring form. He has found the net in back-to-back Gameweeks.

Bruno Fernandes (£10.3m) offers the combination of penalty duties and creative excellence. He is nailed-on for 90 minutes and is the division's best creator by some distance.

Sample Free Hit Squad Structure

A balanced Free Hit squad for Blank Gameweek 34 could prioritise:

  • Premium assets: Salah and Fernandes in midfield, Watkins up front
  • Value picks: Gibbs-White and Rogers offer strong returns relative to their prices
  • Budget enablers: Wilson and Roefs free up funds for premium positions
