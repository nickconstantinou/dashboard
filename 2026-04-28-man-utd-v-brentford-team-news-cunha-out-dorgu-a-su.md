# Man Utd v Brentford: Team News and Lineup Changes

## Manchester United Starting XI

Matheus Cunha is absent from the Manchester United matchday squad for tonight's fixture. His absence opens a spot in the starting lineup, with Amad Diallo (£6.2m) coming into the side. Amad's inclusion represents an opportunity for Fantasy managers at a competitive price point.

Harry Maguire (£4.4m) returns from suspension and takes his place in the starting XI, replacing Noussair Mazraoui in the lineup.

## Substitutes and Returns

On the bench, Leny Yoro has recovered from injury and is available for selection. The young defender provides additional defensive cover for the home side.

Patrick Dorgu (£4.1m) is making his comeback after a three-month absence and features among the substitutes. His return offers manager Ruben Amorim additional options in the wing-back positions.

## Brentford Unchanged

Brentford named an unchanged squad from their Gameweek 33 fixture. The Bees travel to Old Trafford with continuity in their selection.

Meanwhile, Josh Dasilva (£4.9m) has returned to the Brentford matchday squad for the first time in over two years. The midfielder has been sidelined due to a significant knee injury but is now available for selection.
