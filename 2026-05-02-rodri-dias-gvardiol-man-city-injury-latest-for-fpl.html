Man City Injury Latest for FPL Gameweek 35: Rodri, Dias, Gvardiol

Rodri's Fitness Uncertainty

Manchester City's Rodri remains a doubt for the team's Monday trip to Everton in Gameweek 35. The midfielder hasn't trained with the team yet, according to the latest update. Manager Pep Guardiola has stated that it is too early to make a call on whether Rodri will feature in the match.

However, there is cautious optimism regarding the Spanish international. Rodri is dealing with a groin issue but is reportedly making good progress. Pep Guardiola has expressed confidence about Rodri's recovery, suggesting the club remains hopeful about his return.

Ruben Dias Recovery Progress

Ruben Dias continues to recover from a thigh injury and remains unavailable for selection. The Portuguese defender is still out and has not yet rejoined the team for training.

On a positive note, Dias is showing improvement in his recovery. He is not yet with the team, but Guardiola has indicated that he is getting better as he works through his rehabilitation programme.

Josko Gvardiol Long-Term Absentee

Josko Gvardiol is also unavailable for the Everton fixture. The Croatian defender is dealing with a leg injury and is officially listed as a long-term absentee. He remains out of action and is not expected to return in the immediate future.

Manager's Overall Assessment

Pep Guardiola has grouped the three injured players together when providing updates on their recovery. According to the manager, "Him, Josko and Rodri are better," suggesting that all three are progressing positively in their respective rehabilitation programmes.

For FPL managers, Rodri remains the most uncertain of the three for Gameweek 35, while both Dias and Gvardiol are expected to remain unavailable for the trip to Everton.
