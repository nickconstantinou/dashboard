Andersen, Sessegnon, Iwobi: Fulham injury latest for FPL Gameweek 37

Fulham Injury Latest for FPL Gameweek 37

Joachim Andersen Facing Three-Match Ban

Joachim Andersen (£4.5m) is set to miss Gameweek 37 and beyond after beginning a three-match ban following his sending off last weekend. According to manager Marco Silva, "Joachim Andersen is going to be out, as you know." This suspension will carry over into Gameweek 1 of the 2026/27 season, meaning Fantasy Premier League managers with Andersen in their squads will need to plan for an extended absence.

Ryan Sessegnon Remains Unavailable

Ryan Sessegnon (£5.4m) continues his spell on the sidelines with a muscle injury. Silva confirmed that "Sessegnon is still out," indicating the defender remains unavailable for selection this Gameweek. Managers holding Sessegnon should not expect him to feature in the remaining fixtures.

Alex Iwobi Returns to Training

Alex Iwobi (£6.1m) appears close to returning after his hamstring injury. The midfielder "is back in contention after returning to training this week." Silva provided additional context on Iwobi's recovery progression: "Last week, he did some training sessions with us," and this week "throughout the week, he's been working with ourselves." Silva described it as "a good week for him," suggesting Iwobi could be available for selection.

Team News Summary

According to Silva, "All the other players are going to be available for the game." This means beyond Andersen, Sessegnon, and Iwobi, Fulham's squad appears fully fit heading into Gameweek 37. Managers considering Fulham assets should factor in Andersen's suspension when evaluating defensive options, while Iwobi's return provides an additional midfield consideration for those seeking value options.
