# Leeds United Secure Crucial Victory with Stach, Okafor, and Calvert-Lewin Goals

## Match Overview

Leeds United secured another crucial three points on Friday, earning victory through goals from Anton Stach (£4.8m), Noah Okafor (£5.6m), and Dominic Calvert-Lewin (£5.7m). The result pushed Leeds up to 14th in the Premier League table with 43 points accumulated.

However, Leeds saw their clean sheet wiped out in the 71st minute when Loum Tchaouna (£4.8m) found the net past Karl Darlow (£3.9m), denying the team a shutout despite their overall dominance.

---

## Anton Stach's Stellar Performance

Anton Stach delivered a standout display that fantasy managers had been anticipating. The German midfielder made his first start after a three-match absence due to injury, and he marked the occasion in spectacular fashion by netting the opener with a low strike into the bottom corner from 25 yards out. The strike carried an expected goals (xG) value of merely 0.02, highlighting the exceptional quality and difficulty of the finish.

Beyond his goal-scoring contribution, Stach demonstrated his creative prowess throughout the match. He racked up four additional shots on top of his goal, accumulating five total shots, while also creating three chances for teammates.

His all-round midfield excellence has translated into impressive season-long statistics. Stach is now joint-second for chances created this Premier League season, positioned only behind Bruno Fernandes (£10.4m), despite having made just 26 starts in the campaign. This ranking places him among the most creative midfielders available at his price point.

---

## Noah Okafor's Continued Purple Patch

Noah Okafor (£5.6m) continues to deliver exceptional value for FPL managers. The forward was an injury doubt heading into the match but managed to feature and further extended his remarkable scoring run by notching his sixth goal in seven league games. His consistent output makes him an attractive option for managers seeking attacking returns from a mid-price forward.

---

## Dominic Calvert-Lewin's Contribution

Dominic Calvert-Lewin (£5.8m) rounded out Leeds' scoring with a goal that brought his tally to back-to-back home matches. The striker has been showing signs of form at Elland Road, and this brace provides a boost to his owners heading into the run-in.

---

## Defensive Considerations

On the defensive side, Pascal Struijk (£4.4m) ended the match with exactly zero DefCon points. Notably, no Leeds defender exceeded a score of eight in the DefCon metric, suggesting the backline struggled to contain Burnley's attacking efforts despite the overall victory.

---

## Burnley's Struggles

The opposition, Burnley, have now won just one of their last 26 Premier League matches. This dreadful run of form places them firmly in relegation contention and provides context for Leeds' deserved three points in this encounter.

---

## FPL Implications

For FPL managers considering Leeds assets, Stach represents exceptional value at £4.8m given his creative output this season. Okafor at £5.6m continues to justify ownership with his goalscoring streak, while Calvert-Lewin at £5.8m offers potential as a budget-friendly striker option when playing at home.
