Aston Villa v Liverpool team news: Salah + Wirtz are subs

Aston Villa v Liverpool Team News

Match Context

Aston Villa host Liverpool in a fifth versus fourth Premier League matchup. This fixture falls during the penultimate weekend of the 2025/26 Fantasy Premier League campaign.

Liverpool Team News

Liverpool's lineup features one change from their previous match. Joe Gomez (£4.9m) starts, replacing Jeremie Frimpong (£5.7m) in the XI.

Rio Ngumoha (£4.3m) continues his run of starts, while Alisson Becker (£5.4m) does not feature in the lineup.

Key Substitutes on Liverpool Bench

Mohamed Salah (£14.0m) is named among the Liverpool substitutes. Florian Wirtz (£8.3m) also features on the bench but carries a fitness concern. Manager Arne Slot indicated there would be a late decision on Wirtz's availability due to illness.

Aston Villa Team News

Unai Emery has made three changes to the Aston Villa lineup. Pau Torres (£4.3m), Lucas Digne (£4.5m) and Emiliano Buendia (£5.3m) come in for Tyrone Mings (£4.3m), Ian Maatsen (£4.2m) and Ross Barkley (£4.8m).

Despite the changes, this represents a strong XI from Aston Villa. The club faces a Europa League final against Freiburg next Wednesday, though Emery has selected his strongest available side for this Premier League fixture.

Key Players Starting

Morgan Rogers (£7.4m), Ollie Watkins (£8.7m), John McGinn (£5.3m) and Emiliano Martinez (£5.1m) all start for Aston Villa.
