# Fantasy Premier League: Key Injury Updates and Tactical Notes

## Liverpool Return Updates: Isak and Mamardashvili Back in Action

Alexander Isak (£10.3m) and Giorgi Mamardashvili (£4.1m) have both returned from injury, providing Liverpool managers with fresh options. Isak made his return as a second-half substitute, offering a potential goalscoring threat from the bench. Meanwhile, Mamardashvili has already displaced Freddie Woodman (£3.9m) as the preferred starter in goal, suggesting he could be a viable budget option between the posts.

## Florian Wirtz Misses Match Due to Illness

Florian Wirtz (£8.3m) was unavailable for Liverpool's recent fixture after suffering from a stomach infection that affected his ability to train during the week. The midfielder's absence represents a notable omission from the starting lineup, though managers will hope he can recover quickly for upcoming fixtures.

## Salah and Alisson Targeted for Gameweek 37 Return

Liverpool could receive a significant double boost ahead of Gameweek 37, with both Mohamed Salah (£14.0m) and Alisson potentially returning to full fitness. Salah's anticipated return would be particularly impactful for FPL managers, given his premium price tag and consistent attacking output. Alisson's comeback would also provide relief for those relying on the Brazilian goalkeeper.

## Defensive Concerns: Konate Limps Off

Ibrahima Konate suffered a concerning moment during the match, limping off with what he believes was cramp. The defender told the manager that it was cramp, though officials will continue to monitor his condition ahead of future fixtures. Managers holding Konate should watch for further updates on his availability.

## Rio Ngumoha Substituted Due to Cramp

Rio Ngumoha (£4.3m) was removed from the action, but the substitution was precautionary rather than injury-related. The player reported cramping before requesting to come off, with the manager confirming he had adequate contact with the player and decided three minutes prior that it was sufficient reason for his removal. Ngumoha's availability should not be a long-term concern.

## Virgil van Dijk's Near Miss

Virgil van Dijk (£6.1m) came close to adding to his goal tally but was left frustrated after two missed chances. The centre-back ballooned a close-range volley over the bar and then hit the woodwork with a header when he should have scored. Despite the near-misses, van Dijk remains a solid defensive asset at £6.1m.

## Cucurella Shines Out of Position at Chelsea

Marc Cucurella (£6.1m) has been deployed out of position as a winger for Chelsea due to the club's injury situation, and the experiment proved successful. With no fit wingers available, Chelsea turned to Cucurella in an advanced role, and he delivered a positive performance. At his price point, he could offer value if this tactical adjustment continues.

## Pedro Neto and Garnacho Recovery Status

Pedro Neto and Alejandro Garnacho are approaching a return to fitness, though their availability remains uncertain. Officials expressed cautious optimism, stating they are "very hopeful" but cannot confirm exact return dates at this moment. Managers should be prepared for potential late fitness tests when considering these assets.
