# FPL Gameweek 35 Differentials: Three Low-Owned Picks to Consider

Bruno Guimaraes has been a revelation at St James' Park this season, delivering extraordinary returns in home matches. For managers seeking a differential route into Newcastle's attacking assets, Guimaraes offers a compelling alternative to more heavily-owned teammates, combining elite goal involvement with the security of consistent home appearances.
