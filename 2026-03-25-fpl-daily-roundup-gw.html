1. Today's Key FPL News & Injuries

Tottenham Turmoil (Planet FPL)

The biggest shock of the week isn't on the pitch — it's in the managerial dugout. Ange Postecoglou's father passed away, and Tottenham are "keeping cards close to their chest" according to Serge and James on Planet FPL. The pair predict this could trigger a managerial change at Spurs, adding "unpredictability" to a side already in freefall.
